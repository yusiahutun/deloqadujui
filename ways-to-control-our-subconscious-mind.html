Ways to control our subconscious mind

April 19, 2024
blog

Reprogramming the subconscious mind is a very complex process that involves consistently and intentionally influencing one’s thoughts, beliefs, and behaviors. There is no foolproof way to directly control or reprogram the subconscious mind, there are several techniques and practices you can do to have a positive influence on it.

Here are some steps one can take to reprogram their subconscious mind:
1) Understanding and learning about the subconscious mind:

Getting familiar with the idea of the subconscious mind and how it functions. Learning about its role in shaping beliefs, habits, and automatic responses of humans.

2) Identify the limiting beliefs:

One should pay attention to your thoughts, particularly those ones that are self-limiting or negative. These beliefs often reside in the subconscious mind and can hinder personal growth and development. Identify any patterns or recurring thoughts that may be holding you back from reaching your goal.

3) Replacing the negative programming:

Once you’ve identified your limiting ideas, work consciously to replace them with good, empowering thoughts. Repeat positive affirmations, create new narratives, and visualize positive outcomes to counteract the negative programming.

4) The power of visualization and imagination:

Utilize the power of visualization and imagination to create mental images of your desired results. Engage the senses and emotions to make these visualizations more vivid and impactful. Repeatedly visualizing success and positive scenarios, can have an influence on the subconscious mind to work towards achieving them.

5) Hypnosis and subconscious mind, techniques:

Consider exploring hypnosis or other techniques that specifically work in the area of the subconscious mind. Hypnosis involves inducing a relaxed state and providing positive suggestions to reprogram subconscious beliefs. Seek professional guidance or use reputable resources to ensure a safe and effective experience.

6) Repetition and consistency:

People believe that consistency is essential while reprogramming the subconscious mind. Engage in daily practices that reinforce positive thinking, such as repeating affirmations, visualizations, or meditation. Repetition helps to conceptualize new beliefs and overwrite old programming.

7) Emotional release and healing:

Unresolved emotional problems can deeply impact the subconscious mind. Engage in emotional release techniques, such as journaling, therapy, or energy healing modalities, to address any emotional blockages and promote healing.

8) Developing Mindfulness and self-awareness:

Developing mindfulness and self-awareness to observe thoughts, emotions, and behaviors. By being present and conscious about the internal experiences; checking on the negative patterns and consciously choosing more positive responses.

9) Get surrounded by positive influences:

The surrounding environment and interaction with people can have a significant impact on subconscious programming. Being surrounded by positive, supportive individuals can be helpful in uplifting and motivational content.

9) Being patient and persistent:

Reprogramming the subconscious mind takes its time and consistent effort. Be patient and embrace the process. Celebrate small victories along the way, and stay committed to your personal growth and development.

Research done in this area

There is a significant body of researchers who work on the subconscious mind and its influence on our thoughts, behaviours, and well-being. While it’s challenging to provide an exhaustive list of all the studies, but some key areas of research can be highlighted and explored to learn about the concepts and techniques involved in reprogramming the subconscious mind:

  • Cognitive-behavioral Therapy (CBT): CBT is the most widely researched therapeutic approach. It pays emphasizes identifying and changing the negative patterns of thoughts and behaviors. There are many studies and research works who had worked on the effective role of CBT, telling about subconscious beliefs and promoting positive change.
  • Neuroplasticity and Mindfulness: Research works in the field of neuroplasticity have shown that the brain is capable of changing and forming new connections throughout our lives. Mindfulness practices, such as meditation, have been found to promote neuroplasticity and had influence on the subconscious mind positively.
  • Positive Affirmations: Several studies had investigated the effects of positive affirmations on self-esteem, motivation, and overall well-being. Researchers have found that repeating positive affirmations can help to reprogram subconscious beliefs and improve outcomes in various domains.
  • Hypnotherapy: Hypnosis and hypnotherapy have been subjects of scientific investigation. Studies have examined the efficacy of hypnotherapy in addressing about various issues, such as anxiety, phobias, and smoking cessation. These techniques often involve in working with the subconscious mind to cultivate positive change.
  • Subliminal Messages: Some studies have explored the impact of subliminal messages on the subconscious mind. Subliminal messages are those stimuli that are presented below the threshold of conscious awareness. While academics continue to argue the effectiveness of subliminal messaging, certain studies indicate that they may alter specific behaviours and attitudes.
  • Emotional Freedom Techniques (EFT): EFT is also known as “tapping.” It combines elements of acupressure and psychology to address about emotional issues. Research has shown good results in using EFT to reprogram the subconscious beliefs related to stress, anxiety, depression and trauma.
  • Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP): NLP is a field that investigates the relationship between language, patterns of thought, and behaviour. Although there is a lack of effective scientific evidence supporting all aspects of NLP, some studies suggested about specific NLP techniques can be fruitful in reprogramming the subconscious patterns and improving certain outcomes.
