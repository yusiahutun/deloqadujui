James Van Der Beek leads a brilliant Cinco de Mayo-themed workout

January 30, 2024
blog

Jose Cuervo has designed a new workout that, while probably not entirely effective, sure looks like a good time.

In honor of Cinco de Mayo — which happily falls on a Saturday this year — the tequila brand has invented a new exercise plan that marries cardio and strength training with alcohol and guacamole.

Called 'Cinco-Cise,' the made-up training regimen covers hydration, nourishment, and endurance — though it seems designed to prepare a person more for a marathon of the drinking variety than the running kind.

Woohoo! The star notes that the holiday falls on a Saturday this year, meaning a whole day of partying 

Curevo released several new ads featuring actor James Van Der Beek to debut the jokey exercise plan. 

'What's the best time to party on a Saturday? Trick question — it's the whole day!' the star says, pointing out that this year's Saturday Cinco de Mayo is a big deal since revelers don't have to go to work the next day.

'But to get ready for that, you need more than just exercise — you need Cinco-Cise,' he says.

The funny videos demonstrate three different workout moves, each of which will likely lead to negligible weight loss (and possibly even weight gain).

First, for a mix of cardio and strength training, the gym-goers int he video vigorously shake cocktail shakers. They use them like Shake Weights, and also lift up their knees to meet them.

Second, they use tequila bottles and rocks margaritas as free weights for bicep curls, occasionally sneaking a sip.

And finally, van der Beek helps one gym-goer do chips-and-guac sit-ups, offering him guacamole on a tortilla chip every time he makes it up into the sitting position.

'If you want to celebrate all day, you can't forget about nourishment,' he says.

The brand has also released some funny outtakes from the making of the ads, as well as recipes for the big holiday.
