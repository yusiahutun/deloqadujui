A handful of criminals have been snapped sporting some truly bizarre looks after being hauled in front of the camera lens at their local police stations.

While some stand out for their unusual choice of hairstyles and extreme tattoos others, it seems, are simply not that photogenic.

The crooks, all from the Orlando area of Florida, have failed to perfect their picture pose despite almost every one of them having mug shot taken repeatedly.

Hair today: Donald Wait's mugshots show his changing looks as he loses his locks and gains the tattoos

Change of fortunes: Jonathan McMillin looks less cocky as his injuries get worse

Maybe Donald Wait was compensating for losing his previously long locks when he tattooed almost his entire head as his seven mugshots, which date back to 2002, show the progressing hair loss and increasing tattoos on the criminal. The 66-year-old's latest arrest was for having a open container.

Jonathan McMillin looks pretty cocky in his first couple of mugshots but the most recent shot shows him looking less pleased with himself having suffered serious injuries.

The 24-year-old was arrested on charges of having never had a driver’s licence. He was charged weeks earlier for assault and tampering with a witness.


Shock horror: Frances Richards (left) and Rhonda Harrison (right) strike startling expressions

Body art: Roger Whitt, 59, sports different looks in his nine mugshots including this 'superman' disguise

Style-guru: Michael Baumbach has experimented with his hair  including a pink do and an impressive boffant

74-year-old Frances Richards showed her age as she posed for her photo after being charged on one count of burglary and another count of theft.

Distinctive: Kenneth Monte stands out in a crowd

Despite it being her fourth arrest in five months Rhonda Harrison looks surprised to be having her picture taken at the local police station.

Rhonda, 28, was arrested on charges of violating her probation -  it was her third arrest for grand theft last year. 

Luis Munoz, 36, from Orange County was brought in after he was caught trespassing.

Kenneth Monte, 42 (right) has 24 charges under his belt, including criminal mischief, aggressive battery, driving under the influence and reckless driving.

One of Monte's run-ins with the law involved him crashing his car and suffering serious neck injuries, which is why he wears a neck-brace in his mugshot.

It gives him a rather distinctive look.

Kevin Cutlip, 21 (pictured below) has been arrested five times within the last year.

His criminal post has managed to rack up a whopping 28 charges against him, including seven for armed burglary and seven for theft.

But with such a distinctive tattoo across his throat a change of hairstyle does not make much difference to his multiple mug shots.

Distinctive: Kevin Cutlip's unusual neck tattoo remains the centre of attention regardless of changing hairstyles

Say cheese: Alfred Lazzatti (left) tied his beard into pigtails for his mugshot, and Danielle Trudell (right)

Ouch: Bandaged up criminal Jesse Taylor seems seriously unimpressed

Having been arrested seven times, despite her young age, 25-year-old Danielle Trudell could have a wall of shame all to herself.

Since she was first detained aged nineteen, Trudell has been charged with a number of offences.

They have included failing to appear in court, driving with a revoked license and possession of drug paraphernalia.  

Alfred Lazzatti, sported a goldilocks look for his mugshot whereas Michael Baumbach rocked an interesting pink hairdo for his picture.

34-year-old Jesse Taylor does not look best pleased to be wearing his head in bandages for his latest mugshot.

He has been arrested 14 times since last January for a variety of crimes.

His most recent arrest was on five charges: aggravated assault with a deadly weapon, violent disorder against an officer, battery, battery against an officer and drunken disorder.
