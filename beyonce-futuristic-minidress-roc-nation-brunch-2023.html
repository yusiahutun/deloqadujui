Beyonc Is Gorgeous in a Futuristic Minidress for the Roc Nation Brunch

April 06, 2024
blog

Beyoncé made a shining appearance at this year's Roc Nation Brunch.

The superstar attended the annual pre-Grammy event alongside her husband Jay-Z last night, where she wore a futuristic custom minidress by Gareth Pugh. The modern spin on a elevated spacesuit was comprised of a structural white collar rising above the star's shoulders, a skintight bodice with silver panels down the torso and back, and a short bodycon skirt. White fingerless opera gloves ending in folded cuffs above the elbows completed the look.

The "Alien Superstar" singer paired the eye-catching piece with silver platform pumps by Amina Muaddi, and her hair was worn down in a curled wet look. Her accessories included rectangular, white-rimmed sunglasses and a pair of mismatched silver hoops by Messika, one in a starburst pattern and the other featuring a diamond pendant hanging at the center.

While the pop star shared just one pic of the look, in which she poses with one hand resting on a white stepladder, stylist KJ Moody posted shots from a second setup. In these, the out-of-this-world look pops against a hot pink background.

The Renaissance singer also shared a second outfit from her pre-Grammy festivities yesterday, coming a bit closer to Earth in a long-sleeved mock-neck minidress by

Caléchie Custom. The piece evoked molten silver with its artfully-wrinkled fabric and adorning curtain sash, which hung past the length of the bodycon bodice.

Bey finished this look with opaque black tights, open-toed silver platforms, and thin silver hoops from Messika by Kate Moss. She also carried the structural Ruby's Lost Stone bag from Published By.

The record-breaking Grammy nominee is expected to win big at this year's ceremony, where she's up for nine awards including Album of the Year for Renaissance, Record of the Year for "Break My Soul," and Song of the Year for "Break My Soul." Tickets for her highly-anticipated Renaissance World Tour also begin to go on sale next week.
