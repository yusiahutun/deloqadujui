Country star Keith Urban regrets posing with just a guitar to preserve his modesty

With the American Idol season premiere right around the corner, all eyes (and gossip) are on the new judges.

Recently, new judge and Country music superstar Keith Urban dished on why he believes in the show - and while he may be an Idol to many folks, Keith has regrets of his own.

His biggest regret, it seems, is posing nude for Playgirl magazine. Keith recently paid a visit to The Tonight Show, where Jay Leno decided to embarrass Urban a bit, as he whipped out some old Playgirl photos.

In the photos, Keith Urban is wearing nothing more than a guitar, and the Country star was clearly embarrassed by the spread, but tried to make a joke of it.

"I'm just glad that I play guitar and not the harmonica," Urban told Leno. But then Urban got serious and made it perfectly clear that posing for the photo was not something he was proud of.

"There are certain career regrets I have. That falls into that category. That falls into the 'what the hell was I thinking?' category," Keith explained. - SheKnows.com
