Since launching in 2018, luxury stationery company Kinship has already attracted clients such as Christian Dior, House of Hackney and Matilda Goad. Masterminded by sisters Naomi and Alice Howarth, the company is known for its beautiful, bespoke water colours, but also produces woodblock work. Each design is custom, so you can rest assured that your guests won't have seen similar invitations before. As well as invites, the company also offers save the dates, RSVP cards, orders of service and table plans, as well as bespoke maps for destination weddings, monograms and backdrops, plus artwork for fans, napkins and fabrics.
