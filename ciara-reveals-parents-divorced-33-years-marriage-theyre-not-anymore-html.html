She bravely discussed her parent's relationship status for the first time. 

While on RuPaul's talk show, Ciara revealed the bombshell news that her parents have split up after 33 years. 

The host, 58, asked Ciara if her parents, Jackie and Carlton Harris were still married.

'So, actually, I've never said this before, but I can think I can say it [and be] okay within myself,' the singer began, as her voice cracked. 

'My parents were married for 33 years, and they're not together anymore. However, they are both happily married, so that's good.' 

She said that her parent's split occurred a little after she met now-husband, Russell Wilson.

'Imagine, you go for 33 years of Mom and Dad being together, and then when I had my child, my son, my first child, I was like, "I just know Mommy and Daddy that made Baby is what the scenario's supposed to be."'

'So that was like "whoa" when that happened in my life, 'cause this is not what I know.' 

Their break-up happened when the singer was 29, turning 30.
