Joshua Mann | The Methodical Group:A Visionary Leading by Example

April 24, 2024
blog

Over the years, business leadership has witnessed a significant transformation. Presently, leadership is much more than merely command and control. It is more about guiding and inspiring teams amid the continuously changing market dynamics. Today, business leaders constantly have to be on their toes, incorporating technological advancements and pivoting to market shifts. 

Enunciating his views on contemporary leadership, Joshua Mann (Founder and CEO of The Methodical Group), “Essential qualities of a modern leader include emotional intelligence, which allows for effective team management and empathy; visionary thinking, to foresee and navigate future challenges and opportunities; and a commitment to sustainability and ethical practices.” According to him, the ability to inspire and empower teams, while fostering a culture of continuous innovation, is also paramount. 

Where it all started…!

Joshua Mann’s career began in the field of software development, where he had the opportunity to work with some of the world’s largest companies. Many of these companies later became his valued clients. This experience provided him with a deep understanding of the technological needs and challenges faced by large organizations. “Working in this fast-paced, innovative environment was both challenging and immensely rewarding, laying a solid foundation for my future endeavors,” he adds. 

Alongside his professional journey, Joshua Mann was also committed to his education, taking a full load of college classes. His academic journey was marked by exploration and diversity as he attended various schools and switched majors several times. For him, this period was important as it allowed him to gain a broad perspective and understand various fields and disciplines. 

Eventually, Joshua Mann’s career path led him into healthcare, where he identified a critical need for specialized consultancy services. This moment laid the foundation of what would later become The Methodical Group. The ‘Aha Moment’ for Joshua came when he realized that there was a significant opportunity to provide more tailored, efficient consultancy and staffing solutions. His vision was to create a company that addresses clients’ current challenges as well as anticipates future industry trends—thereby providing innovative, cost-effective, and sustainable solutions. This commitment to creating value for clients has been driving The Methodical Group’s success. 

A Versatile Partner

Joshua Mann’s first consultancy firm was established 25 years ago. The Methodical Group, now in its third reincarnation, stands as a prime example of adaptability and continuous growth in response to the ever-changing market demands. At its core, The Methodical Group is an IT services company that primarily focuses on providing bespoke technology solutions that address the specific needs of its clients. The company’s foundation in IT services has been fundamental in shaping the way it approaches client challenges and delivers solutions.

As the company evolved, it recognized an emerging need among its clients—not just for IT solutions but also for comprehensive staffing support. This understanding led The Methodical Group to expand its services beyond traditional IT consultancy. Today, as the company’s client base becomes increasingly diverse, the range of resource types it provides is also expanding. 

The Methodical Group’s expansion into staffing solutions is a direct response to its clients’ growing and varied needs. “It reflects our commitment to being a versatile partner, capable of addressing multiple aspects of our clients’ business challenges,” says Joshua Mann. From offering strategic IT consulting to facilitating staff augmentation and executive search, The Methodical Group has grown to become a multifaceted firm, delivering a comprehensive suite of services designed to empower and advance its clients’ operations.

The Distinguishing Factors

Several factors differentiate The Methodical Group from its peers in the consultancy and staffing industries. Here are some of them:

  • Customization and Client-centric Approach:

The Methodical Group’s customization and client-centric approach ensures that it provides solutions specifically tailored to the client’s requirements. The company does not believe in one-size-fits-all solutions. Instead, it takes the time to understand its clients’ specific contexts and needs which allows it to deliver more effective and impactful solutions. 

  • Commitment to Innovation:

The company is constantly exploring new methodologies and technologies to ensure that its services are not just current, but also future-ready. This forward-thinking approach means it is always a step ahead in identifying and leveraging opportunities for its clients.

  • Specialized Knowledge:

 The Methodical Group’s specialized knowledge, particularly in healthcare, lifesciences, and engineering, provides the company with deep insights that it brings to every project. This expertise enables the company to offer solutions that are both strategic and practical, giving its clients a competitive edge. 

  • Varied Engagement Models:

The company has developed varied engagement models that seamlessly blend staff augmentation, direct placement, and consulting services. The flexibility of these models allows for a sliding scale, tailored to the level of direct management that each client desires. This ability enables The Methodical Group to precisely deliver the level of support and expertise the client needs, whether it is comprehensive management or targeted assistance. 

A Leader Wearing Multiple Hats

As the Founder and CEO of The Methodical Group, Joshua Mann’s role is multifaceted and constantly evolving. Strategic visioning is one of his primary responsibilities. He is responsible for setting the overall direction of the company, identifying growth opportunities, and steering the firm through the ever-changing business landscape. This involves foreseeing future trends and challenges as well as ensuring that the company is well-positioned to leverage these changes.

Joshua Mann’s daily role comprises operational oversight, team leadership, and stakeholder management. He is deeply involved in overseeing the company’s operations, ensuring that its services meet the highest standards of quality and efficiency. Leading and mentoring the team is another critical aspect of Joshua’s role. He believes in empowering the employees and fostering a culture of innovation and continuous learning. Additionally, maintaining strong relationships with clients and other key stakeholders is essential to the company. Thus, ensuring client satisfaction and building long-term partnerships are the top priorities for Joshua.

Source of Inspiration

Every successful leader has a role model whom he looks up to as a source of inspiration. For Joshua Mann, that role model is Sir Richard Branson. “His unorthodox approach to business, combined with his adventurous spirit and unwavering commitment to innovation, has always been a source of great inspiration for me,” he asserts. Sir Branson’s journey, marked by bold decisions and a constant push against the status quo, resonates deeply with Joshua’s values and aspirations as a leader.

Joshua Mann has always been fascinated by Sir Branson’s ability to maintain a human touch in all his business ventures. His leadership style, which emphasizes employee welfare and a positive company culture, has shown that it’s possible to be both a successful entrepreneur and a compassionate leader. His ventures, spanning multiple industries and challenging traditional business models, exemplify the power of visionary thinking and resilience. Joshua further mentions, “Sir Branson’s success with Virgin Group, built on principles of customer service, innovation, and employee satisfaction, has been a guiding light in my journey with The Methodical Group. It’s this blend of daring entrepreneurship and responsible leadership that I strive to emulate in my role as the CEO and founder.”

Milestones and Achievements

Joshua’s illustrious career comprises several significant achievements and milestones. One of the most notable is the growth and evolution of The Methodical Group. For Joshua Mann, transforming the company from a startup focused on healthcare IT into a multi-faceted consultancy and staffing firm has been an incredibly rewarding journey. Being recognized as one of America’s fastest-growing private companies was a testament to the team’s hard work and dedication.

Being awarded the ‘Innovator of the Year’ was another key achievement in Joshua’s journey. He mentions that this recognition was not only a personal achievement but also a validation of the company’s commitment to innovation and excellence. It underscored The Methodical Group’s success in developing solutions that are not just effective but also forward-thinking and sustainable. These milestones reflect Joshua Mann’s commitment to driving innovation, providing exceptional service, and leading a company that makes a significant impact in the industry.

Handling Failures and Setbacks

Handling failures and setbacks has been an integral part of Joshua Mann’s business journey. He believes that failures are not the opposite of success, but rather stepping stones towards it. His approach to tackling setbacks has been to analyze them critically, understand the lessons they offer, and use these insights to improve the company’s strategies and operations. “Resilience in the face of failure is crucial; it’s about bouncing back stronger and more informed,” he adds. 

One of the most important leadership lessons Joshua Mann has learned is the value of adaptability. The business world is constantly changing, and being able to pivot and adapt strategies is crucial for long-term success. Another key lesson is the importance of maintaining a team-oriented approach. Encouraging collaboration, fostering open communication, and valuing each team member’s contributions have been fundamental to building a successful and resilient organization.

“We Pay People to Deliver….!”

Achieving work-life balance is a cornerstone of The Methodical Group’s philosophy, where it has embraced a Results-Oriented Work Environment (ROWE). This approach places emphasis on outcomes rather than the traditional metrics of hours spent at the office. The company believes that people are most productive and engaged when they have the freedom to work in a manner that suits them best.

Joshua Mann often says, “We pay people to deliver, not to sit behind a desk.” This focus on accountability and results allows the team members to manage their time effectively, blending their work and personal lives in a way that promotes overall well-being and job satisfaction. Implementing ROWE has not only contributed to a healthier work-life balance for employees but has also enhanced productivity and job satisfaction across the board.

Personally, this philosophy extends into Joshua’s own life. He strives to lead by example, balancing professional responsibilities with personal interests and family time. His hobbies and interests, which often vary from month to month, tend to involve working with his hands and being outside.

“They currently include restoring an old Jeep, but previously it was hiking and white-water kayaking, and it’s things like these which are integral to my well-being,” he adds. They provide a necessary and rejuvenating break from work, allowing Joshua Mann to return to his professional duties with renewed focus and energy. This balance is essential, as it enables him to maintain a high level of performance at work while also enjoying a fulfilling personal life. Joshua believes that a happy and well-rounded individual is inherently more productive and creative, and this belief is at the core of the work culture at The Methodical Group.

Challenges and Opportunities for the Future

The landscape of business is rapidly evolving, and with it, the challenges and opportunities facing leaders today. According to Joshua, one of the most critical challenges will be navigating the increasing pace of technological change—including how AI will move the industries. Leaders must not only stay informed about these technological trends but also understand how to integrate them into their business strategies effectively. This requires a balance of technical know-how and strategic foresight.

Simultaneously, these technological shifts present significant opportunities for innovation and market leadership. Joshua Mann further adds that leaders who can harness these technologies to enhance operational efficiency, drive customer engagement, and create new business models will position their companies for success in an increasingly digital world.

Advice for the Aspiring

Joshua Mann’s foremost piece of advice to young business leaders aspiring to make their mark is to cultivate resilience. He says, “The path of entrepreneurship and leadership is fraught with challenges and setbacks. It’s your ability to persevere through adversity, learn from your mistakes, and adapt that will define your success. Embrace failures as learning opportunities and use them to fuel your growth. Secondly, deeply understand your market and customers. In a world inundated with data and information, the ability to cut through the noise and genuinely understand customer needs and market trends is invaluable. This understanding should be the foundation upon which you build your strategies and innovations.”

He continues, “Maintain integrity in all your business dealings. The reputation you build as a leader is one of your most valuable assets. Conduct your business with honesty and ethical practices and foster a culture of trust and transparency within your organization. Finally, never stop learning. The business world is constantly evolving, and staying informed and educated is critical. Whether through formal education, mentorship, or self-driven learning, continuously expanding your knowledge and skills will equip you with the tools needed to lead effectively and make informed decisions.”
