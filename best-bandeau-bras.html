Best Padded Bandeau Bra

Lively No-Wire Strapless Bra Bundle

Best Padded Bandeau Bra

Lively No-Wire Strapless Bra Bundle

Credit: Lively

Lively's bra sets have the best value for the best fit, bandeaus included. Light padding, a no-slip silicone lining, and an underwire-free design make these neutral bandeau bras a favorite among reviewers. Here's just a sample of the rave reviews: "This is the first strapless bra I’ve worn and actually felt supported and comfortable in. The band has extra hooks to get just the right fit. The cups have substantial coverage but allow some cleavage as you wish. It was super comfy and stayed on well through a night out!"

Best Seamless Bandeau Bra

Cosabella Free Cut Micro Bandeau Bra

Best Seamless Bandeau Bra

Cosabella Free Cut Micro Bandeau Bra

Credit: Cosabella

The reviews have it: Cosabella's lightweight bandeau bra holds you in place but feels like wearing nothing at all. "I don’t have to adjust it at all throughout the day—it’s like magic how it stays put. Very comfortable and doesn’t show under clothing either," one reviewer gushes. "This might be the best strapless bra I've ever bought, no wires or padding, good shape, and it's TRULY non-slip," another reviewer writes. "No adjustments needed throughout the day." There's one con to keep in mind: Reviewers say it runs small, so consider ordering a size up.

Best Bandeau for Small Chests

Commando Double Take Bandeau

Best Bandeau for Small Chests

Commando Double Take Bandeau

Credit: Nordstrom

Commando's Double Take bra, featuring a lace back and microfiber front, is best suited to shoppers wearing A, B, and C cups. It has light coverage without an underwire for all-day wear. "Comfort-wise, this is as close to perfect as I’ve found. And believe me, I’ve been looking!" one reviewer writes.

Another reviewer is so smitten, they're swearing off other strapless bras. "I ordered [this] to wear when I do not feel like wearing my 'normal' wire strapless bras. I may never go back. This is so comfortable and gives me a good shape."

Best Plus-Size Bandeau Bra

Rhonda Shear Plus Size Ahh Angel Seamless Bandeau Bra

Best Plus-Size Bandeau Bra

Rhonda Shear Plus Size Ahh Angel Seamless Bandeau Bra

Credit: Bare Necessities

In sizes 1X to 4X, this bandeau bra is catered to bigger chests. The four-way stretch microfiber works with the unlined cups and underwire for a supportive fit that also stays in place. One of many satisfied reviewers says this bra changed her outlook on bandeau styles. "I have bought so many strapless bras and they were never comfortable or held up my girls. This bra was perfect—now I am able to wear cute strapless clothes. I will be definitely purchasing more." A few reviewers mention slight slippage, but the overall comfort overshadows that con.

Best Lace Bandeau Bras

Cosabella Never Say Never 3-Pack Flirtie Bandeau Bra

Best Lace Bandeau Bras

Cosabella Never Say Never 3-Pack Flirtie Bandeau Bra

Credit: Amazon

Reviewers have called this set of three lace bandeau bras a "needle in the haystack." They each have an opaque front and semi-sheer back, with silicone lining to hold it in place. For even more support, you can opt for removable pads. Reviewers say these bandeau bras are comfortable enough to become an everyday wear. "I don't even feel the boning, but the bra stays up," one reviewer writes in their recommendation. "I also feel like there is ample stretch to not smash, but enough to provide support."

Best Smoothing Bandeau Bra

Victoria's Secret Smooth Strapless Bra

Best Smoothing Bandeau Bra

Victoria's Secret Smooth Strapless Bra

Credit: Victoria's Secret

The bandeau version of the Love Cloud bra, one of model Adut Akech's favorite styles, is one of the most comfortable on our list. It includes molded, underwire-free cups and a pull-on fit for wearing under any strapless dress or top. Plus, the smoothing fabric is partially recycled. Some reviewers say it runs large, while others say it's a true-to-size fit—order accordingly.

Fleur du Mal Charlotte Bandeau Bra

Best Luxury Bandeau

Fleur du Mal Charlotte Bandeau Bra
