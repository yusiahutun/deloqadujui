Who is Ruben Studdard?

Ruben Studdard was born in Frankfurt, West Germany, on September 12, 1978, and raised in Birmingham, Alabama. He is an American singer and actor. His father, Kevin, a former autobody collision technology teacher, was stationed in Germany with the US Army at the time of his birth. His mother, Emily, teaches in the Birmingham school system.

Ruben’s liking for music began at a young age after listening to his mother’s Donny Hathaway albums, gospel music and Fred Hammond. He then chose to pursue a career in the music industry by majoring in voice studies at Alabama A&M University.
