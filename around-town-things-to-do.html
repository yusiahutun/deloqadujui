Eyes on the Sky in Jensen Beach

February 27, 2024
blog

Seventy-nine drone pilots from across the country will convene in Jensen Beach at Causeway Park for the The Second Annual South Florida Drone Meetup April 2, from 7 a.m. to 4 p.m. Admission to the meetup is free, and participating pilots will enjoy a day of flight and raffle prizes, including drones, GoPros, and more.

The first drone meetup, hosted in 2021, welcomed more than 50 pilots from across the country to Sebastian. The exciting event serves as a chance for experienced and new pilots alike to share their passion for flight.
