10 animal print dresses to go wild for this summer: From M&amp;S to H&amp;M, ASOS &amp; more

February 08, 2024
blog

Animal print is a fashion trend we see year after year, but it's one that we love because it's ever-evolving. Leopard print has been at the forefront for 2023, but we're still seeing plenty of gorgeous zebra and snake prints around too. Whether you're looking for an understated daywear look or a loud colourful piece, there are so many stylish takes on animal print right now.

How to style an animal print dress

Due to its bold design, animal print is most often teamed with neutral colours including black, white and beige. That isn't to say though that an animal print dress wouldn't look great paired with a bold colour for a statement finish.

Royals wearing animal print dresses

Animal print may not be the first style that springs to mind when you think of the royals, but Princess Kate, Meghan Markle, Zara Tindall and Queen Letizia have all been spotted in the versatile print over the years.

The Princess of Wales most recently wore animal print in March, and the royal looked so stylish in the Cefinn green leopard print midi dress teamed with a pair of white heels.
