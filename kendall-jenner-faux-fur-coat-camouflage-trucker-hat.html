Kendall Jenner Pairs a Faux-Fur Coat with a Camouflage Trucker Hat

February 16, 2024
blog

Kendall Jenner's off-duty style just got a lot more wild.

While attending a soccer match at the Parc des Princes stadium in Paris yesterday, the model looked effortlessly cool in a long faux-fur coat in a tawny brown hue from Bottega Veneta. She wrapped the coat over a white T-shirt and capped the ensemble off with a camouflage trucker hat in dark brown and olive green tones.

She shared photos of the unconventional off-duty look on her Instagram Story.

Jenner attended the soccer match alongside big sister Kim Kardashian, who showed up to the game in a black leather biker jacket.

The 818 founder has been on a fashion streak as of late. Most recently, Jenner attended the Oscars after-parties in two glamorous looks.

For the Vanity Fair fete, Jenner walked the blue carpet wearing a sparkling gold-and-black scalloped gown from Jean Paul Gaultier's spring/summer 2008 haute couture collection. The dress featured a body-hugging mermaid silhouette, crisscrossing straps on the back, and petal-like details embroidered onto the bust.

Later in the night, to attend Beyoncé and Jay-Z's party, Jenner changed into an all-brown outfit pulled from Ann Demeulemeester's fall/winter 2023 runway. The look consisted of an earthy brown top covered in shaggy fur and a matching mocha silk skirt that hung low on her hips. She accessorized with dark brown gemstone earrings and wore her long dark hair up in a sloppy high bun.
