Two Pints Of Lager And A Packet Of Crisps won't return as Sheridan Smith snubs co-stars

September 02, 2024
blog

It's not looking good for a Two Pints Of Lager And A Packet Of Crisps revival as star Sheridan Smith still refuses to discuss a could-be reunion.

The finale of the popular sitcom aired nine years ago, and while star Will Mellor, who portrayed Gaz on the show, admitted he would love to see it back on the small screen, fellow cast member Sheridan isn't keen on the idea.

Will told The Morning After with Paul Danan podcast the actress, who played Janet on the programme, hasn't spoken to him in three years and declines his calls when he rings.

"Sheridan just won’t talk about it," he began.
