No matter who you are or what you do, booking a sexy Airbnb rental in the USA can be a great way to escape and rejuvenate. And what better way to get some quality R&R than with your significant other? A quick getaway might not be a weeks-long trip to a different country, but even just shacking up within the United States can do a number on your stress and happiness levels. Let’s face it: We could all use a change of scenery right now.

One of the many benefits of lodging at an Airbnb instead of a hotel is that a lot of them have a full-sized kitchen, so you can whip up a romantic feast together and enjoy it on your outdoor patio or snuggled up by the fireplace with some wine and perhaps a rom-com or two. Whether you have a bonafide love affair with over-the-top honeymoon-suite style abodes or prefer a more minimalist-friendly choice surrounded by nature and scenic views, we’ve rounded a handful of the most romantic Airbnb rentals of 2024 across the USA. Our picks take you to the East Coast, West Coast, and everywhere in between.

Especially with Valentine’s Day around the corner, consider booking any of these 10 options for your next mini-vacation with your partner.

Secluded Hideaway in Park City, Utah

  • Price: $214/night
  • Space: 2 bedrooms, 1.5 bathrooms
  • Superhost: Yes
  • Rating: 4.98 stars (224 reviews)

If you’re looking for the coziest winter getaway, fly (or drive) straight to Park City. You’ll be surrounded by the slopes, yummy food, beautiful views, fun outdoor activities, cute cabins, and more. This secluded hideaway is especially great because it’s not at the heart of bustling downtown Park City. You get all of the space and privacy you could want with your partner. Plus, this Airbnb‘s interior design is simply top-tier coziness. I can’t forget to call out the fully suspended hammock floor and indoor swing chair. There’s no cooler place to spend time with your S.O.

Cabin in Big Bear Lake, California

  • Price: $262/night
  • Rooms: 1 bedroom, 1 bathroom
  • Superhost: Yes
  • Rating: 4.86 stars (637 reviews)

If your partner loves hitting the slopes, look no further than Big Bear. Skiing in California is perfect for cold-weather beginners. It’s not Maine or Wisconsin-level freezing, but it’s still chilly enough to have snowball fights and hot chocolate by the fire. I’ve fallen head over heels for this charming, tree-lined, recently renovated cabin. It’s close to the lake and village, but if you want to be a homebody, that’s fine too! There’s plenty of relaxation to be had in this Airbnb, since there’s a hot tub and fire pit.

Secluded Intown Treehouse in Atlanta, Georgia

  • Price: $389/night
  • Space: 1 bedroom, 1 bathroom
  • Superhost: Yes
  • Rating: 4.95 (526 reviews)

This This secluded hideaway features three secluded suites giving love birds a private sanctuary immersed in lush greenery to bask in nature’s beauty and take a pause from everyday life. The retreat is separated into three distinct areas—Mind, Body, and Spirit, and has a relaxing hammock, luxe Egyptian cotton bedding, and luxe antique furniture. Oh, and the bed even has wheels, so you can sleep indoors or under the stars on the outdoor deck. To book, you do need to message the hosts before booking.

A-Frame Cabin in Shippensburg, Pennsylvania

  • Price: $219/night
  • Space: 2 bedrooms, 1 bathroom
  • Superhost: Yes
  • Rating: 5 (231 reviews)

There’s something so charming about an A-frame home like this one. This Pennsylvania cabin is the perfect destination for those who want to stay in and snuggle with their special someone. Enjoy the view of the woods from inside and outside of the home. If I were you, I’d make a beeline to the firepit and hot tub.

The Tiki Suite in Key West, Florida

  • Price: $552/night
  • Space: 1 bedroom, 1 bathroom
  • Superhost: Yes
  • Rating: 4.89 (432 reviews)

Wow. This swoon-worthy tiki-themed houseboat retreat is literally docked in the water, allowing for a magical aquatic getaway that will help you disconnect, de-stress, and fall in love all over again while you float upon the serene Key West waters. Borrowing the best of boat living with the modern comforts of on-land lodgings, the off-the-grid Tiki Suite is truly an experience you’ll never forget. While you won’t be able to microwave up leftovers or order delivery, the tranquility of the sea and upgraded amenities like Alexa-enabled devices and WiFi will ensure you won’t miss a thing.

Luxury Spa Home in Joshua Tree, California

  • Price: $250/night
  • Space: 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms
  • Superhost: Yes
  • Rating: 5.0 (221 reviews)

This jaw-dropping spa home offers all of the peace, calm, and seclusion you could want during a romantic getaway. Wake up to the California sun’s soothing rays, relax in the pool, tub, or sauna, and then look up at the night sky in the stargazing dome. There’s also a yoga deck, a fire pit, hammocks, an ice bath, and a dining area outside. All of these fab features almost make the Tulum-styled villa with an indoor private cinema seem like the second string of things to do in this abode.

Treehouse at Danville in Geneva, Florida

  • Price: $230/night
  • Space: 1 bedroom, 1 bathroom
  • Superhost: Yes
  • Rating: 4.90 (886 reviews)

Perfect for social distancing and recharging from the work-from-home hustle, this opulent “treehouse” is designed with everything you need for a romantic weekend spent under the stars without the inconveniences of actual camping—it’s even got a trunk elevator, so it’s basically a tree-mansion. Other noteworthy amenities include a wood fire pit, festive tiki hut, high-speed WiFi, and a really neat hot tub created from a DC 10 jet engine cowling. Last but not least, there’s also a real toilet—not composting here.

The Rustic Rose in Fredericktown, Missouri

  • Price: $148/night
  • Space: 1 bedroom, 1 bath
  • Superhost: Yes
  • Rating: 4.94 (173 reviews)

If I could envision my own dream home, it would be molded after the Madonna Inn Hotel in California (my favorite place ever), but The Rustic Rose retreat comes in as a pretty close second. The entire abode is decorated in rich shades of pink and red, textural accents, and plenty of ornate accents. Aside from its kitsch interior, it also offers 33 acres of outdoor adventuring to enjoy as well, including wooded camping trails, access to fishing, and swimming holes.

Domeland: Off-Grid Adobe Dome in Terlingua, Texas

  • Price: $117/night
  • Space: 1 bedroom, 0.5 bathroom
  • Superhost: Yes
  • Rating: 4.95 (907 reviews)

Another great off-the-grid option for a truly peaceful weekend with your lover, this secluded adobe dome in Texas is the perfect quaint rental for enjoying one another under the stars (and some seriously stellar sunsets too) with minimal external distractions and interruptions. It’s just minutes away from Bend National Park (though the host suggests checking the website as it’s reopened with limited capacity only), and while the dome is solar-powered and their shower options are limited (WiFi, laptops, and phones can be charged, but hair dryers are generally a no-go), there are some pretty other impressive amenities you’ll get to take advantage of, including a functional kitchen, cozy linens, and actual peace and quiet. This is the perfect escape for city dwellers who need to recharge and re-center.

South Lake Tahoe Castle in Lake Tahoe, California

  • Price: $765/night
  • Space: 4 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms
  • Superhost: No
  • Rating: 4.72 (110 reviews)

This modern California “castle” features a stunning, full-sized heated indoor pool, a steam room, a dry sauna, a pool table, and a hot tub. Trust me, there will be zero reasons for you and your S.O. to even dream of leaving this romantic retreat. The three-bedroom home gives you and your beaux plenty of space to explore indoors, but it also comes with plenty of outdoor amenities as well, from an outdoor BBQ pit to a spacious deck with a jacuzzi tub and lounge chairs (if it’s not too cold, of course.).
