St George's Day: top 10 English desserts

May 27, 2024
blog

St George's Day is the perfect excuse to make a traditional English dessert – and luckily we're spoiled for choice. To celebrate the feast day of St George on 23 April, we've picked out our favourite sweet treats. Valentine Warner's summer pudding – a favourite of the Duchess of Cambridge – and famous baker Dan Lepard's caramel apple pie might win you over. Or, for those watching the calories, beauty expert Liz Earle's fat-free rose and berry sponge cake has all the taste of indulgence, without the guilt. Scroll down to get the recipe for 10 sweet English classics...

PHOTOS FOR ALL RECIPES

1. Liz Earle's fat-free rose and berry sponge cake Beauty expert Liz Earle's sponge cake is bursting with delicious – and healthy – buttercream and topped with a range of juicy berries.

2. Sherry trifle With layers of custard, fruit, sherry-soaked sponge, jelly and topped off with whipped cream, it's no wonder trifle is a long-standing top runner when it comes to the nation's top desserts.

3. Scones Mouthwatering scones with clotted cream and jam are a traditional teatime treat and national favourite.

4. Apple and pear crumble A traditional apple crumble is both delicious and easy to make.

5. Strawberry meringue roulade with mascarpone The crisp and chewy meringue encases a rich strawberry and mascapone cream filling making this a slice of dessert heaven.

6. Healthy Eton mess Perfect for those of us watching our calorie intake, this classic is made with naturally fat-free Quark.

7. Valentine Warner's summer pudding "For me, this traditional pudding sums up an English summer – nothing can better the flavour of fresh British berries ripened naturally in the sunshine," says Valentine of summer pudding - also a favourite of the Duchess of Cambridge.

8. Plum and citrus custard tart Baked egg custard tart is an all-time classic – this one has a plum and citrus twist for good measure.

9. Dan Lepard's caramel apple pie Apple pie is a winner all year round. Try your hand at Australian baker Dan Lepard's recipe with a drop of caramel.

10. Sticky toffee pudding Satisfy your sweet tooth with this old classic.
