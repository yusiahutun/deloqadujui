Hailey Biebers Vintage Velvet Bodycon Dress Looks Like It Sculpts Her Body

July 12, 2024
blog

Hailey Bieber’s latest monochromatic look is a vintage lover’s dream.

Last night in London, the Rhode founder stepped out with husband Justin in a black bodycon midi dress by Thierry Mugler, hailing from the ’90s. The incredible piece includes black velvet details across the shoulders, bust, and waist, cut into shapes meant to accentuate or sculpt parts of the body. The unique dress also features a wavy, abstract neckline.

Hailey paired the dress with sheer black Calzedonia tights, glossy black pointed-toe Jimmy Choo pumps, and a small black shoulder bag.

Meanwhile, Justin looked cool and casual in a white T-shirt, a hooded leather jacket, baggy light-wash jeans, and white sneakers.

This look caps off a streak of black monochromatic ensembles that Hailey has been pulling off lately.

For instance, last week in New York City, the model headed out with Justin in a black leather set by Brandon Maxwell. The set included a strappy, midriff-baring bandeau top and a matching skintight maxi skirt. She topped off the look with even more black pieces, including a pair of glossy black stiletto boots by Gia Borghini, black oval sunglasses, and a tiny black shoulder bag by Ferragamo.

Prior to that, she enjoyed another night out in New York City while wearing baggy black pleated trousers, a backless black halter top, black pumps, and a black leather shoulder bag.
