For centuries, hairstyles have grown to become the go-to option whenever any lady wishes to transform their look easily. For those who are looking for the next hairdo, be inspired by the following latest hairstyles in Kenya.

Box Braids

Don’t let the simplicity of box braids fool you, they are incredibly versatile and tend to complement every face shape. No matter the colour you can go for long, short or medium length braids. With box braid, you will also not be limited in terms of styling options as you can make your braids into a bun, half up half down design or a ponytail. You can then rock the style to casual events and even to a professional meeting.

Bob braids

It’s impossible to walk through the streets of Kenya and not fin a lady with bob braids. The shoulder-length makes the braids light which puts less stress on your natural hair and are ideal for swimming as they can easily fit in a swimming cap. You can choose to do your bob in micro braids or jumbo and ensure they follow the classic bob style by curling under the chin which helps to frame the face beautifully. Silver and pink bob braid latest hairstyles will surely turn heads.

Senegalese twists

Senegalese twists often rank amongst the most popular and latest hairstyles in Kenya. They provide a fun way to change your braid updos. You can wear your Senegalese twist curled, wrapped up in a bun or flowing freely down your back.

Kinky twist braids hairstyles

Do you want a protective style that complements your face shape? Then why not try the kinky twists. You can wear them up, down or in a funky style that best fits your mood for that day. For a cool colour combination add a few blonde or brown kinky twist to your black hairstyle.

Dookie braids

Have you always wished you could live the 90s trend? Then you can do so with dookie braids. This latest braided hairstyles have come a long way from the simple pulled back hairstyle made of large braids. For a bohemian look, part the dookie braids in the middle and add a flower crown.
