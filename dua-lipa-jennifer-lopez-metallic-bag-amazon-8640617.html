Jennifer Lopez and Dua Lipa Have a Thing for This Metallic Bag Style

February 06, 2024
blog

Metallics aren’t going anywhere any time soon; with celebrities like Anne Hathaway, Katie Holmes, Amal Clooney, and Martha Stewart donning the head-turning trend, it’s difficult to discredit its pertinence. A new generation of stars like Kylie Jenner, Kaia Gerber, and Zendaya are relying on the mirror ball-inspired style, too, proving just how ageless the look really is—and Dua Lipa just furthered my point. 

While out in New York City, Lipa wore a groutfit including a faded denim jacket, slit maxi skirt, sheer tights, and knee-high Bottega Veneta boots. She slipped on a pair of black sunglasses and clung on to the silver Gucci Horsebit Chain Shoulder Bag, worth $3,490—which isn’t much if you’re comparing it to a $12,450 Birkin bag…but to me, that’s a really pretty penny. 

5 Silver Metallic Purses

Speaking of Birkin bags, Jennifer Lopez (who almost always has one in hand) is also on board the shiny purse train. In a Coach campaign, the singer posed with a super glitzy Tabby purse and flaunted it again at New York Fashion Week. Not only is it very similar to this Coach Tabby Wristlet, but her styling choice demonstrates just how noteworthy a shimmering purse can be.

Motleader Metallic Shoulder Bag

Whether you opt for a $19 Motleader lookalike or an $84 JW Pei version, it’ll glimmer under the light and cast a gorgeous brilliance. This effect instantly makes any outfit more glamorous, providing jeans, T-shirts, and even sneakers a key piece to play off of; though, futuristic bags work just as well with elegant dresses, sophisticated kitten heels, and billowy maxi skirts, too. The secret is to style around the bag—not against it. 

JW Pei Joy Shoulder Bag

With that said, I’d go as far to say that metallics are taking the place of neutrals. Of course, classic black bags and white purses aren’t going anywhere, but lustrous silver is more versatile than you might realize; it coordinates with bright spring and summer colors, pops against darker shades, and adapts to its surroundings thanks to a mirrored finish. Metallic silver additionally combines both edgy and timeless characteristics, making the style great for a plethora of aesthetics.
