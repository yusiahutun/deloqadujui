Cheryl Cole draws comparisons to Beyonc Knowles with new single Crazy Stupid Love

March 09, 2024
blog

    Cheryl Cole’s new single has been compared to Beyoncé Knowles' anthem. The singer – who shot to fame as part of the band Girls Aloud – released her latest music offering on the radio airwaves Monday morning as she visited Nick Grimshaw’s BBC Radio One breakfast show. As soon as her track played the judge became a trending topic in the UK on Twitter and fans gave their comments on the tune. The Geordie star looked great as she did the London promotional rounds in an all-white ensemble.

    Cheryl Cole

    Cheryl, 30, donned a see-through top and jeans, plus a black and white heeled shoes which co-ordinated with her ebony bag and large sunglasses. She revealed that her new song would soon be available for listening on Sunday as she took to Twitter. "If you want to hear the new single #CrazyStupidLove I'll be on @BBCR1 with @grimmers tomorrow at 7:45am," she posted.

    Before that she let followers know that she was ready to make her comeback to the music scene by sharing a shot to accompany her track.

    Cheryl Cole at Radio One

    "I am so excited to be able to share some music news with you!" she exclaimed. "My new single Crazy Stupid Love featuring Tinie Tempah is released July 20th! You can hear it on radio from Monday #CSL."Cheryl was recently at the Cannes International Film Festival with the new man in her – Jean Bernard "JB" Fernandez-Versini. The handsome Frenchman has been dating her for the past few months and the 33-year-old runs pop-up restaurant and celebrity-hangout Cosy Box at Cannes. Having been educated at New York University, JB is something of an international jetsetter – splitting his time between Los Angeles, New York and France.
