Since the year began, Kenya has recorded murder cases that have grabbed the nation's attention.

TUKO.co.ke highlights some of the heartwrenching cases that saw innocent Kenyans lose their lives to people known to them;

1. Rita Waeni found dead in Roysambu

On Sunday, January 14, the body of a young woman was found dismembered at an Airbnb in Roysambu.

After investigations, it was revealed that the murdered lady was Rita Waeni Muendo, a 20-year-old Jomo Kenyatta University of Science and Technology (JKUAT) student.

The slain university student was killed by a man who was captured on a CCTV camera booking the room.

The family of the murdered JKUAT student disclosed that her killers demanded a ransom of KSh 500,000 before killing her.

Detectives probing Rita's murder recovered critical items from the house of Nigerian suspects who have been arrested in connection with the brutal murder.

2. Starlet Wahu found stabbed to death at an Airbnb

The gruesome murder of popular socialite Starlet Wahu at an Airbnb in Nairobi's South B estate shocked Kenyans.

According to police, the 26-year-old checked in at Papino Apartments in South B Estate on Wednesday for a fun-filled night alongside her partner, John Matara.

Wahu's last moments alive were captured on a CCTV camera as she and Matara checked into the building.

While processing the scene, detectives found a Samsung phone, a bottle of alcohol, condoms, marijuana and a kitchen knife suspected to be the murder weapon.

According to the Nation, the crime scene investigators also found an HIV test kit whose results were negative.

Starlet's body was taken to City Mortuary for a postmortem, while Matara, who is the prime suspect, was traced to Mbagathi County Hospital, where he had gone for treatment.

3. Sarah Wairuri's body found in water tank

In Nyeri county, police are investigating the mysterious death of a 47-year-old woman who was found dead in Kiruga village, Karima ward, in Othaya Constituency.

On Sunday, January 21, the body of Sarah Wairuri was found stacked inside a 3000-litre plastic water tank at her home, four days after she went missing.

Her daughter became suspicious after she noticed an unpleasant odour and flies coming from the tank, forcing her to alert the authorities, who recovered the body of Wairuri from the tank full of water.

In his statement, Nyeri governor Mutahi Kahiga called on the police to speed up the investigation in order to arrest those behind the mysterious death of the 47-year-old woman.

"Sarah Wairuri of Karima Ward is no more. Sad as it may be, Sarah went missing last week only for her to be discovered dead in a water tank at home. The circumstances surrounding this death are unclear. We collaborated with Faith Mwaura to put up a decent house for her. I have asked the police to urgently and expeditiously investigate this death. Heartfelt condolences to her children, family, friends and relatives. May Sarah rest in eternal peace," Kahiga said.

4. Grace Wangari Thuiya stabbed to death in Githurai 44

In other sad news, a woman died after sustaining severe injuries following a fight with her male lover in their residential place in Githurai 44.

Grace Wangari Thuiya died on January 20 at the Kiambu Level Five Hospital, where she had been rushed for treatment; she bled excessively.

The Star reported that the 24-year-old had an altercation with her partner, Clinton Mwangi, before she was stabbed in the head.

Police responding to the couple's neighbours' distress calls rushed her to the nearby Imani Hospital before her referral to the level five facility, where she was pronounced dead on arrival.

5. Homa Bay woman found murdered in her kitchen

The brutal murder of a 25-year-old woman who was found in a pool of blood in her kitchen left Homa Bay residents shocked.

Christine Auma was allegedly killed by unknown assailants on Wednesday, January 17, when she was cooking at her home in Kamwala village, Ndhiwa constituency.

According to Assistant Chief Milton Kawino, Auma’s husband and other relatives were in their main house awaiting supper when they heard a loud bang from the kitchen direction.

When they rushed to the scene, they found her head missing, with no trace of the suspects.

6. How police the body of a woman dumped on the roadside in Juja

A middle-aged woman was found murdered under mysterious circumstances in Juja, Kiambu county.

Citizen Digital reported that on Wednesday, January 17, the body of the woman was found dumped in the Gwa-Kairu area of Juja.

What Juja police boss said about the murder Juja sub-county Police Commander Michael Mwaura said detectives from the Directorate of Criminal Investigations (DCI) based in Juja visited the scene and are trying to establish who was responsible for the incident.

7. How Kisumu teacher was killed by husband

In another sad incident, police are investigating the alleged murder of a 33-year-old secondary school teacher, allegedly by her husband.

The teacher from Obwolo Secondary School in Kisumu was allegedly attacked and killed by her husband at her Oboch pub in Nyakach sub-county.

Confirming the incident, North Nyakach division assistant county commissioner Darwin Orina said the police had commenced an investigation.

Orina stated that the suspect, the lady's husband, was also in custody, adding that the law would not be lenient to anybody involved in the heinous act.

"The suspect is already in custody, and investigations have commenced. Any other person who will be found to have been involved in the matter will be apprehended," he stated.
