JoJo Siwa is giving Christina Aguilera with 'stunning' hair transformation

May 31, 2024
blog

JoJo Siwa is looking a little different over on her Instagram as she debuts her ‘hair transformation’ and fans, including some very famous ones, just can’t get enough.

The former reality TV star took to her social media to show off her new look, as many were comparing JoJo Siwa to pop icon Christina Aguilera, in her Xtina 2000s era.

JoJo Siwa debuts hair transformation

Taking to her 11.2 million Instagram followers, JoJo posted a picture of her new hair extensions, and gorgeous blonde locks, which she simply captioned: “Surpriseeee.”

The music star posted a photo dump of pictures and videos showcasing her long extensions with blue highlights, the front pic being a selfie in the front seat of her car.

In one of the videos, the 20-year-old can be seen admiring her new look in the mirror, as her colorful closet can be seen in the background.

The change comes after the star recently debuted her first tattoo over on the ‘gram, and explained the ‘deep’ meaning behind it.

Fans are ‘obsessed’ with the new look

Upon seeing JoJo’s new look, celebrity faces, and fans rushed to the comments of the post, including music star Demi Lovato, who commented: “LOVE,” with three fire emojis.

“It’s giving 2000s Christina Aguilera’s XTINA era,” penned one fan.

Another agreed as they exclaimed: “All I can think of is the Xtina dirty era!!!!!! Love it!!!!!!”

“Obsessed with you,” one commented.

One fan wrote: “Why is it giving 2014 vibes? btw I love the hair super super cute.”

“Stunning,” said another.

JoJo went ‘brunette’ at the start of Summer

This isn’t JoJo’s first transformation this Summer. Back in June, the Dance Moms star switched up her golden locks for a two-toned blonde and brunette hairstyle.

Taking to her Instagram stories, the TikTok star, who is set to make her return to reality TV, posted a video of her new look which she captioned: “Got some cool new hair I’m obsessed with.” However, the short hair still remained, unlike this time.

The star is best known for her high pony, but as of late, has been slaying the bob. Could the extensions mean the return of the signature style?
