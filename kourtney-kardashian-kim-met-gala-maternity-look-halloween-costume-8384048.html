Kourtney Kardashian Wore Sister Kim's 2013 Met Gala Maternity Look as a Halloween Costume

September 20, 2024
blog

Halloween may still be a few days away, but Kourtney Kardashian has already won best costume. 

On Friday, the Poosh founder got into the spooky spirit early by dressing up as her younger sister Kim, wearing the exact same floral Givenchy gown she wore to the 2013 Met Gala when she was pregnant with North. "Freaky Friday," Kourtney — who is expecting her first child with husband Travis Barker — captioned a slideshow of a snapshots of her sporting the maternity look, which included a long-sleeved rose-patterned dress with a mock neckline, built-in gloves, and a thigh-high slit. Like Kim, Kourtney slipped on a pair of heeled sandals in the same print, and styled her long dark hair in a sleek low ponytail with a middle part. A dark vampy lip provided the finishing touch to her glam. 

Kourt's choice in costume is seemingly a reference to the sister's recent feud about copying each other. Kourtney first accused Kim of her copying her dolce vita lifestyle, to which Kim fired back to say that she "stole my fucking wedding country."

"I got married in Italy. Am I saying you copied me by getting married in Italy? Who performed at my wedding? Who performed at Kourtney's wedding? Andrea Bocelli," Kim said in a confessional during an episode of The Kardashians. She added, "You stole my fucking wedding country and my wedding performer. Andrea Bocelli is my favorite male singer of all time, but I am copying her dolce vita lifestyle?"

When it came to Kourtney's Halloween costume, however, both sisters were in on the joke, and Kim reposted the look to her Instagram Stories with three hand-clap emojis.

Well played, Kourtney. Well played.
