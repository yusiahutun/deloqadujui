Thrive Causemetics Bigger Than Beauty Skincare Launch 2023

July 23, 2024
blog

You may know Thrive Causemetics from their cult-favorite Liquid Lash Extensions Mascara, which set the standard for tubing mascaras, or Brilliant Eye Brightener. Staying true to their name, Thrive Causemetics is actually built on a charitable foundation. With every product sold, the brand donates a product or monetary donation to one of the 500 charities they work with, and its mission has become equally as integral to the brand as its formulas. The brand’s next chapter follows suit as Thrive Causemetics Founder and CEO Karissa Bodnar launches her first official foray into skincare, called Bigger Than Beauty, with the same charitable initiative and intentional formulas.

“I wanted to be super thoughtful about putting out another skincare line,” Bodnar tells BAZAAR.com. “My goal was luxurious, clinically proven skincare.” While Thrive Causemetics, which launched in 2014, has dabbled in skincare before, Bigger Than Beauty dives deeper into the skincare world but from a new angle after customer feedback. “When we first launched our skincare in 2019, we had an insanely amazing response,” Bodnar tells us. “But people wanted to see more sustainable packaging from us. The packaging today has a 40 percent reduction in plastic from Thrive Causemetics products.”

This time around, Bodnar collaborated with cosmetic chemists and dermatologists to create what she coins as luxurious, clinically proven skincare at an affordable price point. “I myself am a product formulator, and I have developed products like Liquid Lash Extensions on my own,” Bodnar explains. “But for Bigger Than Beauty, we brought in an amazing team of experts so that the skincare formulas could be efficacious and clinically proven." Like to its sister brand, Bigger Than Beauty will be vegan and cruelty-free. “Not only do I believe in vegan cosmetics the better option when it comes to skincare, but I believe in being kind to the animals and the earth,” Bodnar adds.

Shop Bigger Than Beauty

Bigger than Beauty consists of a 10-piece collection, including the Defying Gravity Eye Lifting Cream, Liquid Brilliance Super Serum, Moisture Flash Conditioning Toner Mist, and Bright Balance Hydrating Cleanser, the latter which Bodnar praises for removing Thrive Causmetics’s fan-favorite mascara. “Thrive Causemetics is known for our waterproof makeup that does not come off–until you want it to," she says. "The cleanser is the ultimate remover for our budge-proof cosmetics and we sought out the approval of dermatologists, and most importantly, ophthalmologists to make sure you can put this cleanser in your eye and it will not burn.” Bodnar designed the products to be versatile, including the cleanser, which she also uses as a treatment. “I love to leave it on for a few minutes in the shower as a mask to let the pineapple enzymes brighten and tighten skin,” she adds.

“I wanted Bigger Than Beauty to provide a super simple skincare routine that can be done in two minutes if you’re short on time or used in various ways depending on your needs,” she says.

Bigger Than Beauty products range from $18 to $59 and are available now at thrivecausemetics.com.
