Zendaya Wore Sequined Spiderweb Tights on the Red Carpet

May 02, 2024
blog

There's no doubt that Zendaya slays every red carpet she steps onto, and this time she even dressed on-theme for the occasion. On Sunday, the actress attended the Spider-Man: No Way Home London photo call wearing an appropriately festive outfit.

Zendaya opted for an Alexander McQueen blazer dress and tights combination that had our spidey senses tingling. Her sheer black tights were embellished with beaded detailing that resembled spiderwebs and came down over her shoes, which included an illusion-creating heel. Her double-breasted suit jacket dress was also adorned with the web-patterned embellishments, and she accessorized with large silver spiderweb earrings that included a pearl spider and all.

For her glam, Zendaya opted for slicked back, wet-looking hair that flowed down her back in curls, and exaggerated black eyeshadow to accentuate her brown eyes.

The actress was accompanied by her boyfriend and star of the movie Tom Holland on the carpet, who wore a white button down, black trousers, and a matching leather jacket (all Celine).

The recently confirmed couple addressed their height difference last week during a hilarious interview on The Graham Norton Show saying that while those two inches don't bother them in real life, it did cause some complications on set when it came to stunts. "There's a particular stunt where Spider-Man swings us on top of a bridge, and he places me there. He's supposed to gently rest MJ on the bridge and walk away from her. Because of our height difference… we were attached, so I would land before him," she explained.

"My feet obviously hit the ground before he does," she added with a giggle.
