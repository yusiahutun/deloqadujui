Sebastian Vettel leaves fans baffled as F1 star shows off completely different hairstyle

February 14, 2024
blog

Former F1 world champion Sebastian Vettel has left fans stunned after showing off his new hairdo ahead of the upcoming 2022 campaign.

Vettel is currently on the books of Aston Martin, who finished seventh in last year's F1 championship.

But the four-time champion had fans buzzing on social media after his appearance at the brand's new car launch on Thursday.

Vettel was sporting a much fuller, curly mane during the event - a far cry from the recent short style that the German has been seen with in recent years.

And fans wasted no time in flocking to social media to speak about Vettel's new look.

As well as unveiling Aston Martin's sleek new model, the AMR22, ahead of the new F1 campaign, Vettel also spoke out in defence of Michael Masi.

The F1 chief has been under fire ever since the conclusion to the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix; where late drama and a controversial decision from the Australian enabled Max Verstappen to pip Lewis Hamilton to the world title.

But Vettel has backed Masi by insisting that the onus should be on making the rules clearer rather than continuing to chastise the Race Director.
