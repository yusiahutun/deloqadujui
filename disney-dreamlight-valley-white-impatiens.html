Disney Dreamlight Valley White Impatiens: How To Get

June 03, 2024
blog

White Impatiens in Disney Dreamlight Valley are a rare type of flower used for certain quests and for gifting to characters to raise friendship levels. To find out where you can find this rare flower in Disney Dreamlight Valley read here.

Disney Dreamlight Valley: Where To Get White Impatiens

White Impatiens exclusively and randomly spawn in the Forgotten Lands. These flowers are rare but not impossible to find and are recognisable from the image above as the only white flowers to spawn in the area.

To gain access to the Forgotten Lands you’ll need to pay 15,000 Dreamlight to get rid of the giant Night Thorns blocking the area and 10,000 Star Coins to repair the teleport shaft. You must also have access to the Sunlit Plateau biome as this is the only means of accessing the Forgotten Lands. The biome is situated at the topmost left of the map in the purple region.

Disney Dreamlight Valley White Impatiens: Uses

Like with other forage items in Disney Dreamlight Valley, White Impatiens can be sold for Star Coins or given to characters to raise friendship levels. They can sell from between 40-85 Star Coins each.

Once you have collected as many White Impatiens as possible from the Forgotten Lands, you’ll need to wait for them to respawn after a day. This means waiting in real-time as the in-game clock replicates the time zone you are playing in.
