The Best Underboob Bikinis For A Certifiably Sexy 2023 Summer

Summer heat always results in fewer layers of clothing. It seems like these days, however, that celebrities barely want to wear anything at all. Between the sheer dresses, exposed thongs and peekaboo bras trending right now, it’s clear that sometimes less really is more. Naturally, the most underrated summer look for 2023 is the underboob bikini trend. Don’t believe me? Kendall Jenner and Lily-Rose Depp have already taken it upon themselves to model some of the best underboob bikini looks of the season. I dare you to literally follow suit.

An underboob bikini follows a simple concept: underboob is hot, so show it off on purpose. These swimsuit tops are deliberately cut to expose a playful amount of underboob—they’re sexy but they’re still tasteful. While sideboob is totally the move for an evening look or going-out top, underboob works best with a bikini. For the big-busted ladies out there (myself included), underboob has pretty much always been an inevitable part of wearing a bikini. This is our time to shine. For everyone else, welcome to summer’s best trend. If you’re eager to join the #freethenip movement but unfortunately don’t live near a topless beach or a private pool, the underboob silhouette is the next best thing.

  • Kendall Jenner for FWRD

    Kendall Jenner showed off the perfect example of an underboob swimsuit in her latest campaign for FWRD. In the campaign image, the model can be seen posing in a chocolate brown suit from the brand Di Petsa.

  • Lily-Rose Depp in “The Idol”

    Jenner isn’t the only celebrity embracing the trend. If you tuned into the premiere of HBO’s new series, The Idol, this past week, you may have noticed a fair share of underboob via Lily-Rose Depp, the show’s star. As Depp’s character suggests, underboob gives off an ultra-cool, daring popstar vibe—AKA the exact energy we are trying to channel this summer. 

    While there are plenty of swimsuits with intentional underboob cutouts, the underboob trend is also pretty easy to DIY. Simply tie the neck of your triangle bikini top a little tighter or pull out that one top in your drawer that’s just a little too small.

  • Twisted Underbust Bikini Top

    If you love Kendall Jenner’s underboob look, you can copy her style exactly with this chocolate brown bikini. The cut-out at the bottom and swimsuit elastic will give your boobs a nice lift while still showing a tasteful underboob. Be sure to pair it with the matching bottoms. 

  • Cut Out Cross Front Bralette Bikini Top

    This bikini from Target is the perfect summer shade of orange. The front of the bikini can be crisscrossed or just worn regularly depending on your style (and willingness to deal with strange tan lines). 

  • Campbell Plissé Underwire Bikini

    I love this swimsuit from Frankies Bikinis because it looks like someone cut out a suggestive slice of an underwire bra. It’s supportive, sexy and structurally interesting. What more could you want in a trendy piece? This option comes in three colors and looks great with the skimpy bottoms. 

    $80.50 (was $115)

  • Beaded Wrap Bralette Bikini Top

    This swimsuit can be an underboob bikini if you want it to be. All you have to do is adjust the straps to sit slightly higher on your shoulders or order it in a size down for a more exposed fit. It comes in three colors and the bottoms have adorable beaded ties. 

    $14 (was $20)

  • The Roy Top

    This bikini was made for the underboob trend. It comes in four color options so don’t be afraid to mix and match the top and bottom. 

  • Metallic Swim Micro Scoop Bikini Top

    This scoop bikini top is considered “micro” which is basically a translation for “it gives you underboob.” The metallic fabric makes it so cute for a pool party or bachelorette situation. It comes in five colors and is available in sizes XS-4X. 

  • The Carra Top

    This swimsuit hits two trends in one. It gives you a bit of subtle underboob and then emphasizes the shape with the double-layered straps. I love that the matching bikini bottoms have the same double-layered effect. 

  • Strappy Bikini Top & Bottom Set

    This yellow swimsuit comes in a set which means you can get the whole look for just $30. The top features a strap to help keep your underboob secure and perky. 

  • Sea Bikini Top

    This halter bikini top will give you just a little bit of underboob action in the open center. This is a more discreet way to rock the trend and the suit can easily be adjusted to accommodate different environments depending on how underboob-friendly they are.
