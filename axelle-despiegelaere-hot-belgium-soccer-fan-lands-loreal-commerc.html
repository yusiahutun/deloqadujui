Axelle Despiegelaere, HOT Belgium Soccer Fan, Lands L'Oreal Commercial

blog

Move over, Katherine Webb.

And make way for Axelle Despiegelaere!

This 17-year old Belgium soccer fan has gone viral after cameras caught her watching her team’s World Cup games in Brazil.

Despiegelaere caught the attention of L’Oreal, who quickly signed the beauty up for a “beauty tutorial” commercial that has been viewed over 437,200 since it went live on Tuesday.

 

The footage (below) depicts Despiegelaere in a beauty salon, receiving a blow dry and a full hair treatment.

L’Oréal is the world’s biggest beauty company, but sources say it has not yet signed Despiegelaere to be an official brand ambassador.

Still, we’ve got to imagine it’s just a matter of time. Hot, sexy, gorgeous times…
