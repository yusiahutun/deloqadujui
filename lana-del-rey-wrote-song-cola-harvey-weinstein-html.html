Lana Del Rey wrote the song Cola about Harvey Weinstein

June 27, 2024
blog

Lana Del Rey did write her 2012 song 'Cola' about Harvey Weinstein according to a new report.

An entertainment industry insider confirmed the longtime rumor to Page Six, saying the singer was inspired to write the track after Weinstein began pursuing a relationship with her around that time.

On the track, Del Rey sings: 'I got sweet taste for men who're older / It's always been so it's no surprise / Harvey's in the sky with diamonds / And it's making me crazy / All he wants to do is party with his pretty baby.'

The 'Harvey' line was later removed - reportedly at the disgraced executive's insistence.

'Harvey went bananas and insisted Del Rey change the lyrics, which she did, removing Harvey's name, so the line now goes, "Ah he's in the sky with diamonds and he's making me crazy,"' said the source.

The written lyrics may have been charged, but the singer still clearly says 'Harvey' on the track.

The song was released in November 2012 on Del Rey's third EP 'Paradise,' and Del Rey and Weinstein were spotted lunching in Paris at L'Avenue earlier that year during Haute Couture Fashion Week. 

Del Rey was later hired by Weinstein Company to pen two songs for the 2014 film Big Eyes, which earned her a Golden Globe nomination.

'Lana Del Rey exemplifies strength, femininity, and independence as an artist,' said Weinstein of the singer at the time.

On the track, Del Rey at one point croons: 'Come on baby, let's ride / We can escape to the great sunshine / I know your wife and she wouldn't mind.'

That would seem to suggest that there was a relationship between Del Rey and 'Harvey,' but that is not the case according to the entertainment insider. 

The source said that Del Rey 'rebuffed him, she had a boyfriend.' 

And on the final verse Del Rey sings: 'Drugs suck it up like vanilla icys / Don't treat me rough, treat me really niceys / Decorate my neck / Diamantes ices.'  

Three's company: The singer would later be hired by Weinstein Company to write two songs for the 2014 film 'Big Eyes' (pair above in January 2015 with Amy Adams)

Sweating it out: Weinsten joined her on her 2013 tour (above in June in a backstage video posted by her opening act Kassidy)

Del Rey and Weinstein have been spotted together a number of times since their 2012 lunch in Paris, hanging out together at the 2014 Cannes Film Festival, while promoting 'Big Eyes,' and at the 2016 pre-Oscar party hosted by the Weinstein Company.

That last event shows Weinstein awkwardly looking off in the distance as his smiling wife Georgina Chapman greets Lady Gaga and Del Rey.

Weinstein also hung out backstage during Del Rey's 'Paradise' tour in early 2013, with a video posted by her opening act Kassidy showing the executive roaming around in black sweatpants and shirt. 

Reps for Del Rey did not respond to a request for comment.
