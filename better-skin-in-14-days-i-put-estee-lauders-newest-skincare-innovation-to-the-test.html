I Put Este Lauder's Newest Skincare Innovation to the Test

July 08, 2024
blog

Re-Nutriv has been Estée Lauder's crown jewel since 1956, when Mrs. Estée Lauder herself dared to create the world’s first luxury creme. Her intent: to launch a skincare line featuring the best of everything—science, technology, texture, packaging. More than half a century later, Re-Nutriv remains Lauder's most luxurious and scientifically advanced lineup. The latest addition to the collection, Ultimate Diamond Brilliance Soft Creme Moisturizer, is the culmination of 15 years of skin longevity research and 23 worldwide patents.

The Science Behind the Soft Creme Moisturizer

Genevieve Monsma

Re-Nutriv has been the primary beneficiary of Estée Lauder's extensive skin longevity research, with its formulas getting upgrades over the years as discoveries are made. The brand's breakthrough SIRTIVITY-LP™ is the star ingredient in the Ultimate Diamond Brilliance Soft Creme Moisturizer; it works, according to the brand, by "turning on" skin cells' own natural ability to repair and renew. The patented technology in SIRTIVITY-LP™ is designed to help aging skin cells function at an optimal level again, enabling skin to act, look, and feel younger longer than previously thought possible—the very definition of longevity.

Estée Lauder Re-Nutriv Ultimate Diamond Transformative Brilliance Soft Creme Moisturizer

Estée Lauder Re-Nutriv Ultimate Diamond Transformative Brilliance Soft Creme Moisturizer

The Ultimate Diamond Brilliance Soft Creme Moisturizer also contains Black Diamond truffle extract grown in the south of France, which is harvested at the height of the season and immediately extracted to preserve potency. These truffles are graded "Extra Class" (the best you can get), and in the brand's in vitro testing they've proven to help improve the skin's "natural energy and visible vitality."

The Ultimate Diamond Brilliance Soft Creme Moisturizer is deeply nourishing yet fast-absorbing—and gets to work quickly. In Estée Lauder's clinical testing, the formula was shown to yield visible improvement in the appearance of dark spots, fine lines, post-acne marks, smoothness, firmness, and radiance in just 14 days.

How I Tested It

Genevieve Monsma

Before

I put myself on a strict skincare diet of Re-Nutriv products (other than SPF) for two weeks, using the new Re-Nutriv Ultimate Diamond Transformative Brilliance Soft Creme Moisturizer, Re-Nutriv Ultimate Diamond Transformative Brilliance Serum, Re-Nutriv Ultimate Diamond Transformative Brilliance Eye Serum, and Re-Nutriv Hydrating Foam Cleanser twice a day.

During the first week of testing, I found that the Soft Creme Moisturizer was just what my dehydrated, end-of-winter skin was craving. The formula is light but velvety and left my complexion plump and dewy, but not slick. I also used it on my chest and hands, in hopes that it might fade some of my sun spots. The Hydrating Foam Cleanser easily erased my makeup at night, and the two serums—one for the face, one for under the eyes—have textures that are what I'd call Goldilocks-approved (neither too thick, nor too thin). By week's end, my skin did feel noticeably softer and had a subtle glow, the kind I usually only enjoy on vacation (or after a week of good sleep, something that's harder to come by now that I'm menopausal).

In the second week, the Soft Creme Moisturizer was really put to the test, as I was on the road for my son's hockey tournament. This meant poor sleep in a (non-luxury) hotel, salty restaurant-chain food, late nights, and hours spent in dry, icy rinks and on the highway—none of it exactly a recipe for radiant skin. But having the Re-Nutriv products along added a touch of luxury to my pre-bedtime ritual (I even caught my husband using the cleanser; he raved about the foaminess). Each night, my skin drank up the moisturizer, and by morning it had me at least looking as if I'd had a decent night's sleep.

Genevieve Monsma

After

On day 14—back in my own bathroom and lighting—I took a close look in the mirror and really did see, and feel, a difference in the overall tone of my skin. The color appeared brighter (like it does after an invigorating yoga class), the texture was more even (my pores, in particular, were less apparent), and my skin felt smoother to the touch. I also noted that the lines around my eyes were softer. Finally, while I don't believe 14 days is enough time to undo decades of sun damage (all incurred before 1994, when I finally got wise and started wearing sunscreen), I did notice that the spots on my hands and chest looked a little lighter.

I have every reason to believe these results will only improve with continued use. I also believe the Soft Creme Moisturizer is a worthy option for anyone seeing multiple signs of aging and seeking a luxurious, efficacious skincare solution (and what woman isn't?). I was pleased with the improvement in my fine lines, skin texture, and sun spots—and grateful for the little radiance boost, even if I was only showing it off at the rink.
