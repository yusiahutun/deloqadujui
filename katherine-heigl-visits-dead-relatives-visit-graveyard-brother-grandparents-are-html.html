Katherine Heigl visits dead relatives on visit to graveyard where brother and grandparents are

March 18, 2024
blog

She spent the weekend visiting family in Buffalo, New York.

And Katherine Heigl made sure she kept up with everyone...including her long dead grandparents and brother Jason.

The 39-year-old and her musician husband Josh Kelley, 38, headed over to Forest Lawn and took plenty of Instagram snaps of the Grey's Anatomy alum posing with the beautifully maintained headstones.

Keeping up with her family: Katherine Heigl visited Forest Lawn Cemetery in Buffalo, New York, at the weekend to visit her brother Jason Heigl who died in a car crash at age 16 in 1986 

One very plain stone said, simply: 'Jason Debus Heigl, 1970 - 1986' a memorial to her brother.

He  died at age 16 after he was thrown from the back of a pickup truck in a car crash while out for lunch with high school classmates and succumbed to his injuries.

The couple were somewhat surprised when Josh 'found his own grave...which was weird,' she wrote along with a snap of him sitting on the grass, leaning on a rough-hewed stone with his name chiseled on the front.

There was no sign of the couple's three children: adopted daughters Naleigh, nine, who hails from South Korea, and Adalaide, six, from the U.S., or their 17-month-old son Joshua who were probably with Katherine's family.
