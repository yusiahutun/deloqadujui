Matt Lauer's allegations

On November 29 2017, NBC News revealed the termination of Matt's employment. An unidentified female NBC employee reported that Matt harassed her during the 2014 Winter Olympics in Sochi, Russia.

The woman claimed that the harassment went on even after returning to New York. NBC management claimed that it was unaware of Matt's previous allegations even though this was highly disputed by a former NBC News correspondent, Linda Vester.

In 2019, in his book, Catch and Kill: Lies, Spies, and a Conspiracy to Protect Predators, American journalist Ronan Farrow cited multiple sources who stated that NBC News was aware of Lauer's misconduct beforehand. The revelation created a huge grudge between Ronan Farrow and Matt Lauer.
