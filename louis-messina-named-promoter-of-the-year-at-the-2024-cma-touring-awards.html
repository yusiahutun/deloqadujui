Louis Messina Named Promoter Of The Year At The 2024 CMA Touring Awards

May 11, 2024
blog

NASHVILLE, TN (CelebrityAccess) – The Country Music Association staged the annual CMA Touring Awards in Nashville on Monday night, honoring the professional achievements of the country music industry.

Hosted by country legend Keith Urban, the awards gala recognized the top touring professionals in the U.S. for 2024.

The Messina Group’s Louis Messina was named as the talent buyer/promoter of the year while WME’s Jay Williams was named as the Talent Agent of the Year.

Meg Miller was recognized as the Tour Manager of the Year for her work with Lainey Wilson while Red Light Management’s Mandelyn Monchick was honored as the Manager of the Year.

The late Ron Baird, former agent and head of CAA’s Nashville office, was posthumously honored with the CMA Touring Lifetime Achievement Award. Marc Dennis, agent and co-head of CAA’s Nashville office as well as Baird’s stepson, accepted the honor on behalf of Baird.

Stars on hand in support of their teams included Chris Stapleton, Thomas Rhett, Lainey Wilson, as well as members of Old Dominion.

Additionally, the CMA took the opportunity to unveil its new Touring Mentorship Program, intended to help early-career touring professionals to receive support from established members of the community.

As part of the six-month program, participants will engage in monthly meetings along with hadowing and projects in the field. The Touring Mentorship Program will cover seven key roles in the touring industry: Tour Manager, Band Leader/Musician, Production Manager, Production Assistant, Front of House Engineer, Monitor Engineer and Lighting Director/Designer.
