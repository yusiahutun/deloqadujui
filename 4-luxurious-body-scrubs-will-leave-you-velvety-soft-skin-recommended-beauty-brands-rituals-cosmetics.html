4 luxurious body scrubs that will leave you with velvety soft skin, from recommended beauty brands Rituals Cosmetics, Ouai, Drunk Elephant and Fresh

1. Rituals Cosmetics’ The Legend of the Dragon

Rituals Cosmetics’ The Legend of the Dragon golden body scrub (US$26) is formulated with antioxidant-rich plum and soothing cedarwood, and is packaged in a striking design that references the imperial dragons of China. Available at rituals.com.

2. Ouai Scalp & Body Scrub

The top-selling Scalp & Body Scrub (US$45) from American beauty brand Ouai contains a heavenly blend of sugar and coconut oil. Use it to gently exfoliate and cleanse your hair and skin. Available at sephora.hk.

3. Drunk Elephant Sugared Koffie

Drunk Elephant’s Sugared Koffie almond milk body scrub (US$34) provides moisture and keeps the skin remarkably smooth. We love that it’s powered by nourishing almond milk and coffee seed extract. Available at sephora.hk.

4. Fresh The Original Brown Sugar Body Polish

If you’re able to get your hands on it, we recommend trying Fresh’s The Original Brown Sugar Body Polish (US$45). The in-demand exfoliator is made with several essential oils and brown sugar crystals to massage and buff dry spots. Available in Hong Kong at Lane Crawford.
