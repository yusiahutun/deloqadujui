20 Funny, Yet Inspirational Quotes by Nicki Minaj That Solidifies Her Queenship

June 30, 2024

Besides being a rapper, singer, and mother, Nicki Minaj is many things, with her funny side standing out in her competitive world of showbiz.

From dealing with haters to navigating her most difficult life challenges, Nicki Minaj has a tip or two to dish out, having walked through such situations with grace throughout her music career.

Perhaps this attribute of the rapper contributes to making her the ultimate rap queen amid a sea of contenders. Although others have come close to beating her out of her title, Minaj always has a way of standing tall, cementing her stance in the industry.

In October 2021, her graceful personality came through again during an episode of “Drink Champs” featuring singers Quavo and Takeoff. The Migos members were asked to make a pick between Nicki Minaj and her closest rival, Cardi B.

After mulling it over, both rappers chose the former, despite their link to Cardi B and her husband, Offset.

While some consider this a confirmation that Offset has indeed fallen apart from the rest of the Migos, others deem it as the ultimate proof that Nicki was the undisputable Queen of rap. 

Whether one considers her discography, style, fandom, influence or the overall population of music lovers, one can’t help but fall in with the second category. That said, here are twenty inspirational sayings by Nicki Minaj that prove her royalty status.
