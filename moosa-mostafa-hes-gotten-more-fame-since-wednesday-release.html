Who Is Moosa Mostafa? 

Moosa Mostafa is a talented young British actor best known for his role on Netflix’s “Wednesday.” He was born in 2009 in Warwickshire, England, but his parents are Arabs and are initially from Dubai, the United Arab Emirates.

While Mostafa is best known as Eugene from “Wednesday,” it is not his first role. He is also known for playing Nas, an intelligent but socially awkward boy, on the Netflix sci-fi adventure series “The Last Bus.” 

He also played a minor role in the 2018 film “Nativity Rocks!” as a St. Bernadette’s Class member. According to reports, he is currently managed by The Artists Partnership, a London-based talent agency.

Moosa Mostafa Career

Mostafa’s acting career kicked off in 2018 when he bagged a role in the film, “Nativity Rocks!” as a student in St. Bernadette’s class. He made his TV debut when he featured in “The Last Bus” (2022).

He joined the “Wednesday” cast in 2022 as Eugene Ottinger. At the time of this writing, Mostafa has appeared in more than 15 episodes of television throughout his developing career. He attends Warwick School.
