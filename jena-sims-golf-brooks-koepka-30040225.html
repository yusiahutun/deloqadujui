Jena Sims, the gorgeous wife of top golfer Brooks Koepka, has been regarded by some as the 'world's sexiest LIV Golf WAG'.

The actress and model has built a reputation for herself as one of supreme stunners in the golf world as she's captivated her audience through her social media posts.

The 34-year-old has 270,000 followers on Instagram and she isn't afraid to show off her spectacular body in a whole host of raunchy bikinis.

She has since founded a non-profit organisation called Pageant of Hope that hosts beauty pageants for children with cancer and other disadvantages.

Her dalliance with Koepka began in 2017 before the pair got engaged in 2021 and married a year later in June as they near the one-year anniversary of their wedding.

The couple announced earlier this month (May 3) that they're expecting a baby and more happy news for the Koepka clan occurred this month as the golf ace won the PGA Championship yesterday (May 21).

The Florida native secured his third PGA title and his fifth major championship overall after finishing nine under par following tense battles with Viktor Hovland and Scottie Scheffler as the pair finished joint-second.

Koepka also made history as he became the first player in the LIV Golf League to win a PGA title just a month after he finished second in the Masters, which was won by Jon Rahm.

After securing three majors in three years, Koepka was on a red-hot streak in 2017 and 2018 as he became the world number one in the latter year but a four-year drought without a major followed, before his long-awaited victory yesterday.

Following his runners-up finish in April, the 33-year-old said he believed he choked under the pressure, but the Florida native certainly came good this time round.

"This is probably the sweetest one of them all because all the hard work that went into this one, this one is definitely special," said Koepka.
