Janhvi was born and raised in Mumbai, India, and is the daughter of famous Bollywood actress Sridevi and film producer Boney Kapoor. Before pursuing a career in acting, Janhvi studied acting at the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute in Los Angeles, California. She made her Bollywood debut in 2018 with the film Dhadak, which was a remake of the Marathi film Sairat. Janhvi is a trained classical dancer and has been learning Kathak since the age of four. She is also a fitness enthusiast and often shares pictures and videos of her workout routines on social media. Janhvi has several upcoming projects, including the films Dostana 2 and Takht. She is the brand ambassador for several leading brands, including Nykaa, Nirma, and Boney Kapoor's production company, Bayview Projects LLP. Janhvi has won several awards for her performances, including the Zee Cine Award for Best Female Debut in 2019. She is known for her fashion sense and has been featured on the cover of several leading fashion magazines. Janhvi is often compared to her mother Sridevi for their similar looks and acting skills.
