14 Hallmark Movies You'll Love If You're Obsessed with 'When Calls the Heart'

August 09, 2024
blog

Anything for Love

Courtesy of The Hallmark Channel/Crown Media United States

These two will do just about anything for love—and that includes telling a lie or two about their careers. While Katherine (Erika Christensen) is an assertive executive pretending to be an assistant and Jack (When Calls the Heart's Paul Greene) is a male nurse claiming to be a doctor, these two will have to learn to be honest with each other when their worlds suddenly collide.

Campfire Kiss

Courtesy of The Hallmark Channel/Crown Media United States

When single mom Dana (Danica McKellar) struggles to enjoy herself on a camping trip with her son, she finds a new appreciation for adventure and the great outdoors when she meets fellow single parent and "nature boy" Steve (Paul Greene).

Chance at Romance

Courtesy of The Hallmark Channel/Crown Media United States

When hopeless romantic Samantha (When Calls the Heart leading lady Erin Krakow) travels far to meet her new email pen pal in person for the first time, she learns she's been corresponding with his son—not him. But once these two are forced to spend some time together, they realize that perhaps they're meant to be together after all.

A December Bride

Courtesy of The Hallmark Channel/Crown Media United States

After accidentally breaking up her engagement, Seth (When Calls the Heart star Daniel Lissing) agrees to go with newly single Layla (Jessica Lowndes) as her date to her ex-financé's Christmastime wedding. When Seth tells everyone they're engaged, these two are forced to keep up the charade until their lie turns into something more.

Finding Father Christmas

Courtesy of The Hallmark Channel/Crown Media United States

When Calls the Heart cast mates Niall Matter and Erin Krakow star opposite each other in this sweet Christmas movie. While Miranda goes off in search of her birthfather, she meets and befriends Ian, a local handyman. With his help, she begins to learn more about her past while they both rediscover the magic of the holidays.

Frozen in Love

Courtesy of The Hallmark Channel/Crown Media United States

When these two first meet, arrogant hockey player Adam (Niall Matter) is struggling to overhaul his image while sweet bookstore owner Mary (Rachael Leigh Cook) finds herself stuck trying to save her failing shop. They quickly learn they're total opposites, but they might also be exactly what each other needs.

Growing the Big One

Courtesy of The Hallmark Channel/Crown Media United States

Cue up your DVR on #ThrowbackThursday with this 2010 movie starring Shannen Doherty and When Calls the Heart's Kavan Smith. In this festive fall film, radio talkshow host and city slicker Emma has to come up with a quick way to pay off her grandfather's mortgage after she inherits his pumpkin farm. Thankfully she has local handyman and neighbor Seth to help her grow her big idea.

Meet My Mom

Courtesy of The Hallmark Channel/Crown Media United States

When Dana (When Calls the Heart's Lori Loughlin) is introduced to Sgt. Vince Carerra by her 10-year-old son, the two instantly hit if off but struggle to find a way to make their relationship work as his next deployment approaches.

My Favorite Wedding

Courtesy of The Hallmark Channel/Crown Media United States

The optimistic and romantic Tess (Maggie Lawson) is less than thrilled to be stuck running wedding errands with Michael (Paul Greene), the cynical best man, prior to her best friend's nuptials. But when the wedding weekend in Chicago comes to an end, Tess is left questioning everything she left behind.

Perfect Match

Courtesy of The Hallmark Channel/Crown Media United States

Wedding planner Jessica (Danica McKellar) and event planner Adam (Paul Greene) may not see eye-to-eye when it comes to coordinating their clients' big day, but they'll quickly learn that opposites attract.

The Perfect Bride

Courtesy of The Hallmark Channel/Crown Media United States

If the Coulters are your favorite Hope Valley couple, then you must watch this movie starring When Calls the Heart fan favorites Pascale Hutton and Kavan Smith. In this cute film, fitness trainer Molly and photographer Nick instantly hit it off when they meet at a wedding—until she runs into him again with his bridezilla fiancé.

Stop the Wedding

Courtesy of The Hallmark Channel/Crown Media United States

Anna (Rachel Boston) and Clay (Niall Matter) team up to take down her aunt's and his dad's impromptu Las Vegas wedding, but find themselves a bit distracted when they begin falling for each other.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Wedding Bells

Courtesy of The Hallmark Channel/Crown Media United States

To help out their strapped-for-cash friends, designer Molly (Danica McKellar) and chef Nick (Kavan Smith) volunteer their services to help them plan the wedding of their dreams. While they join forces to makeover a run-down venue, they discover just how well they work together.

The Wedding March

Courtesy of The Hallmark Channel/Crown Media United States

If seeing sheriff Bill Avery and outlaw AJ Foster team up during season 4 left you wanting more, then this movie (and its two sequels) are a must-watch. When high school sweethearts Olivia (Josie Bissett) and Mick (Jack Wagner) meet again just before she's about to wed her new man, they'll have to figure out if they were meant to be after all.
