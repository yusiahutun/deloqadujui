She's certainly not shy, but even exhibitionist Nicole 'Snooki' Polizzi will likely be left red faced by new pictures doing the rounds of the internet.

Photos which appear to be of the Jersey Shore star disrobing have been circulating online.

While the pictures certainly look like the brunette at first glance, it's still unconfirmed if they're legitimate or not.

If it looks like Snooki: Sexy shots that may be of the reality TV star have been going viral on the Internet

In one photo a woman, who appears to be the 24-year-old, is wearing a white robe.

The grainy photo is of the girl, wearing bright green nail polish, looking seductively into her own mobile device, as if she's taking a self-portrait.

The next shot shows the robe being opened to reveal private parts, which have been crossed out by black bars.

The photos appear to have been taken in a hotel  room as the sheets look like hotel-issued linens and there is a room service tray on the bed.

The third picture in the series is a close up of a girl's full nude body. Again, the private parts have been black-boxed out.

If the seductive photos are real, they must be at least four or five months old because Snooki is heavily pregnant.

Her first child with fiance Jionni LaVelle is due in September.

It is not known how the pictures, which first surfaced on blog Egotastic, were obtained.

Snooki herself has released prank photos in the past.

In March, the $30,000-per episode reality TV star tweeted a funny photo of herself with a fake huge belly as if she were carrying triplets.

'OMG I finally sprouted a bump!' she joked.
