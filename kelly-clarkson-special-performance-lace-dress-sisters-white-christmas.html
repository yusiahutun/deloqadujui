Kelly had fans actually seeing double, as she performed alongside Jessi Collins a magical rendition of Rosemary Clooney's Sisters performance, from 1954's classic White Christmas. Plus, yes, Rosemary is George Clooney's aunt!

The duo recreated the original sequence perfectly, both wearing Tiffany blue, waist-cinching dresses with a fitted lace bodice and a voluminous tulle gown. They paired their outfits with matching silver patent leather mary jane heels and a bold red lip, as they held a fluffy feather fan in the same blue color.

In a cheeky twist, they were later joined by The Kelly Clarkson Show crew members Jaco Caraco and Jason Halbert, the latter of which is Kelly's music director and has worked with her since her American Idol days, after she won its debut season in 2002.
