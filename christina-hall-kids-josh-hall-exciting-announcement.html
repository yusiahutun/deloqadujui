Christina Hall makes exciting announcement about 'kids' with Josh Hall

September 02, 2024
blog

The Christina in the Country star and her husband live in Franklin, Tennessee

    Christina Hall and her husband Josh are celebrating the arrival of two kids – of the goat kind, that is.

    The realtors and TV stars have several goats on their small farm, and now it appears they've got offspring.

    The star took to her Instagram story on Thursday evening to share the news.

    "We have baby goats," she wrote alongside a photo of two kids and an older looking goat standing near the chicken pen on their farm. The former host also tagged her husband in the post to share in her excitement.

    DISCOVER: What Christina Hall has said about having another baby - in her own words

    Whether the couple have welcomed more than two baby goats to their farmyard home in Tennessee remains to be seen, although hopefully the 39-year-old and her husband will be inclined to share more about the adorable young animals soon on social media.

    The family's announcement about the happy arrivals comes at a great time for the whole Hall family, as last week they were having to deal with the sad passing of one of their chickens as a result of a predator attack.

    Speaking about her choice to move to the family's current impressive home in Franklin, Tennessee, which sits on a stunning 23 acres of woodland, the California-born TV personality told in 2021: "I fell in love with Tennessee when I took the kids out there in October to visit my friend and nutritionist Cara Clark who had recently moved out there.

    "Tennessee is gorgeous and the people out there are so nice," she continued. "Being out there on all that land reminded me of my favorite childhood memories, which were staying summers on my grandparents’ farm. I felt so carefree and loved taking out their quads and hanging with my cousins on all the land."

    Christina lives with her husband Josh and her three children: Taylor, 12, and Brayden, seven, who she shares with her first husband and former Flip or Flop co-host, Tarek El Moussa; and Hudson, three, who she shares with her second husband Ant Anstead.

    While Josh does not currently have any children of his own with Christina, the 42-year-old appears to be a caring step-father to the TV personality’s three children from her previous two marriages.
