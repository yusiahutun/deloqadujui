Buffalo Bills safety Damar Hamlin suffered cardiac arrest last week during a Monday night showdown against the Cincinnati Bengals.

Ten minutes of CPR needed to be performed on Hamlin before he could ultimately be rushed to a local hospital.

Luckily, thanks in no small part to some quick-thinking from a heroic member of the Bills, Hamlin now seems to be on the road to recovery.

Unfortunately, in a weekend where everyone was honoring and paying tribute to Hamlin, the Pittsburgh Steelers decided to go a different route.
