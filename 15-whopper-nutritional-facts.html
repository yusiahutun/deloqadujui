FAQs

1. How many calories are in a Whopper?

A Whopper contains approximately 660 calories.

2. How much fat is in a Whopper?

A Whopper has 40 grams of fat, including 11 grams of saturated fat.

3. Is the Whopper suitable for vegetarians?

No, the Whopper contains beef as its main ingredient and is not suitable for vegetarians.

4. Can I customize my Whopper to make it healthier?

Absolutely! You can ask for no mayo, extra lettuce, or even opt for a lettuce wrap instead of the bun to make it lighter and more nutritious.
