12 Zodiac Jewelry Brands to Make Your Star Sign Shine

March 03, 2024
blog

The zodiac jewelry trend shows no sign of slowing down and, frankly, we’re here for it. Not only is it a luxe way for astrology enthusiasts to rep their rising, moon, and sun signs, but zodiac jewelry’s rise in popularity means there are more options than ever to choose from. Designers like Monica Rich Kosann and Erin Fader are incorporating astrological symbols into dainty stackable necklaces, signet rings, and statement pieces at both fine and fashion jewelry price points so you can wear your sign on your sleeve no matter your budget. 

Ahead, we’ve rounded up the best zodiac jewelry brands and pieces for every sign and aesthetic. Whether you’re looking for zodiac jewelry that will spark your sign’s specific style preferences or just want a shiny way to work birth charts into the conversation on your next Hinge date, the dozen zodiac jewelry brands ahead have got you covered. 

Baby Gold

As its name suggests, Baby Gold specializes in high-quality white, yellow, and rose gold jewelry. Baby Gold is big on personalization the scale of its zodiac jewelry offerings reflects the brand's focus on truly unique pieces. There are Leo-worthy statement signet rings and medallion necklaces, nameplate styles for individualistic Aquariuses, and minimal stud earrings for subtlety-loving Capricorns. One especially Baby Gold unique piece allows shoppers to rep their sun and moon signs via a delicate charm bracelet.

Pandora

If you’re looking to add zodiac charms to your preexisting jewelry collection, consider Pandora your one-stop shopping solution. The jewelry behemoth not only has both gold-plated and sterling silver charms in its expansive collection but offers classic celestial dangling styles as well as edgier, abstract pieces perfect for sliding onto the chain of your choice. Want to rep your astrological inclinations without revealing your sign? Fret not. There’s a charm for that. 

No.13 Jewelry

No.13 Jewelry keeps its eyes and designs turned up to the stars and its zodiac necklaces, bracelets, and rings draw inspiration from the source of astrology itself: the stars. These star-themed bracelets, rings, and necklaces feature the constellation associated with each of the 12 zodiac signs. No.13's heavenly zodiac pieces come in recycled 9ct solid gold or recycled sterling silver and feature ethically sourced gemstones and conflict-free diamonds. For even more personalization, No.13 offers a birthstone version of its Constellation necklace, allowing shoppers to pair their birthstone with their star sign.

Erin Fader Jewelry

Like many of the brands on our list, Erin Fader Jewelry's zodiac collection allows shoppers to choose between medallion and signet styles. Unlike other brands, Erin Fader Jewelry adds an exciting bit of edge to its signet styles thanks to the contrast created by vintage stones and 18k gold. Both the brand's art deco and medallion-style zodiac necklaces are available as single pieces or as part of a layered set, the latter of which takes the guesswork out of the somewhat tricky layered necklace trend.

Milamore Fine Jewelry

Handmade in Japan, Milamore is a fine jewelry brand that brings its sleek, modern aesthetic to its zodiac jewelry collection. Rather than literal depictions of each zodiac symbol, Milamore's zodiac charms and earrings channel the essence of the astrological signs with nods to each zodiac sign's associated animals or elements with sculptural fluidity.

Satya Jewelry

Another starry-centric zodiac jewelry brand, Satya Jewelry goes beyond traditional zodiac medallions with its gold-plated and sterling silver zodiac necklaces with gleaming enamel, unexpected pendant shapes, and a vintage aesthetic that veers into the abstract without ever losing its star sign focus.

Rellery

In addition to offering zodiac medallion pendants, Rellery utilizes charms and personalization in its astrological bracelets and necklaces. You can add multiple zodiac sign charms to wear your full birth chart on your sleeve or incorporate birthstones, letters, or a slew of other symbols. In addition to 18K gold plated and sterling silver charm necklaces and bracelets, the brand also boasts adorable zodiac stud earrings. Yes, we want them all. 

Monica Rich Kosann

The enamel used in Monica Rich Kosann’s zodiac jewelry collection is truly stunning: rich, vibrant, and deliciously glossy. Shades of pistachio, sage, burnt umber, midnight blue, and carmine serve as the backdrop to the brand’s chic astrological sign charms which can be purchased with or without a chain. Not big on bright colors? Monica Rich Kosann also offers its zodiac charms in solid gold. 

Alex + Ani

Looking for delicate zodiac jewelry that won't break the bank? Alex and Ani has you covered. Its zodiac cuff and charm bracelets as well as individual zodiac charms look lovely when worn alone or stacked. With all zodiac pieces available for less than $50, we recommend going for the stacking approach.

Abbott Lyon

Abbot Lyon's zodiac jewelry pieces are made from layering so you can rep your moon, sun, and rising signs all at once without deviating from a sleek and minimal aesthetic. As a bonus, its double zodiac sign necklace and bracelet, available in silver, yellow, and rose gold, allow you to feature two astrological signs, giving you twice as many ways to show off your astrological identity.

Azlee

Designer Baylee Ann Zwart crafts designs with intention, which is evident in her fine jewelry line, Azlee. Using the finest materials, Zwarft creates gorgeous pieces that harken back to ancient times, giving the pieces a modern heirloom effect. Stunning necklaces, pendants, and rings are adorned with constellations overlayed in diamonds that add a dreamy quality to the pieces.

Miansai

This unisex brand specializes in fine jewelry for the everyday, modern minimalist while still maintaining an accessible price point. Their zodiac pieces depict simplistic renderings of each sign for a classic, easy-to-wear look perfect for any starry night.
