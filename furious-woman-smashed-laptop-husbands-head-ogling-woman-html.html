Furious woman who smashed laptop on her husband's head because he was ogling another woman

Tiffany McLemore, 30, has been identified as the woman who unleashed a vicious tirade on her husband and smashed a laptop over his head on an American Airlines flight from Miami to Los Angeles on Sunday

A California woman who unleashed a vicious tirade on her husband and smashed a laptop over his head on an American Airlines flight has been identified by police.   

Tiffany McLemore, 30, launched the merciless attack after accusing her husband of 'looking at another woman' on a plane preparing to depart from Miami to Los Angeles on Sunday. 

In footage filmed by fellow passengers that went viral on social media, McLemore was seen screaming at the man and calling him a 'n*****' multiple times before flight attendants intervened and asked him to move to another seat, away from her.  

As he made his way down the aisle, McLemore chased him down and struck him over the head with a laptop, injuring a flight attendant and another passenger in the process. 

Another flight attendant witnessing the chaos warned McLemore: 'You're going to be arrested for assault' and she snapped back: 'Fine, whatever' before collecting her belongings and storming off the plane.

Police arrived at the gate and tried to locate the enraged passenger, but their search 'yielded negative results', according to report obtained by the Miami Herald.  

The husband spoke to authorities and said he did not want to press charges. 

He ended up taking a later flight back to Los Angeles and his wife's whereabouts are unknown.
