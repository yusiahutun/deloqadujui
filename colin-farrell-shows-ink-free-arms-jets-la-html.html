He once had six tattoos including a large tribal design that stretched from his shoulder down his arm.

Almost ink-free: Colin Farrell's large tribal tattoo on his arm and shoulder had almost gone while the cross on his other arm was just visible when he jetted into LA in a vest on Thursday
