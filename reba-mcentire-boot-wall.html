Take it from Reba McEntire: Every woman needs a good pair of cowgirl boots. If you have any doubt that the country music superstar knows her boots, just take a peek into the custom closet where she keeps all 85 (!) of her favorite pairs.

Thanks to a recent collaboration with Justin Boots, fans can even wear Reba's favorite footwear fashions. The Reba By Justin collection includes 15 modern takes on classic western boots and casual shoes.

Courtesy of Reba by Justin

A cowgirl at heart, Reba has always been fond of rugged shoe styles.

"Justin Boots have been a staple in my closet since my barrel racing years in Oklahoma," the country star said in a press release. "I'm thrilled to add my personal touch to a brand that is known for quality craftsmanship and timeless style."

"This collection is for all those strong, spirited women and cowgirls at heart who have followed my journey throughout the years," she continued. "This opportunity couldn't have been a more natural fit and creating the line has been too much fun!"

Of course, she looks adorable rocking a pair from her own line!

"I wanted a variety of boots that I could wear on stage," she explained in an interview with People. "Then I wanted a pair of boots for the girls to run barrels in. Then I wanted some sneakers for when they get through loading their horse up in the trailer, take their boots off, get their tennis shoes on and drive home ... I was going by what I like, and hopefully that’s what everybody else would enjoy too!"

Reba's go-to design? The Chelsea boot, which she named after her granddaughter. Shop a few of our favorite Reba By Justin styles below, and be sure to check out the entire line at rebabyjustin.com.
