Top Taiwanese baby names and meanings

April 01, 2024
blog

Are you looking for the perfect name for your baby boy or baby girl? Why not try a Taiwanese one. Taiwanese baby names have a beautiful flow when pronounced, and most of them translate to beautiful meanings.

In Taiwan, one’s surname comes before their given names. For instance, TSANG Ting-Hua. The surname usually has one syllable, and the rest generally have one to two syllables. It is common for the younger generation of Taiwanese to have an English name. Sometimes, these names are direct translations of their Taiwanese names, but they can also be chosen for aesthetic purposes. A classic example is, Susan Ting-Hua TSANG. It is common in Taiwanese culture for women to maintain their family names when they marry another Taiwanese.

Naming is generally patrilineal, with children often adopting the surname of their fathers. For many Taiwanese, their names are either poetic or are instilled with some significant meaning.
