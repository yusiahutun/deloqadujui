Do group chats increase Snap score: what you should know

blog

Snapchat is a standout and one of the most popular social platforms today. It has a range of features, the most cryptic being the Snap score. This feature baffles many users as it's hard to define and calculate. So, do group chats increase Snap score?

Snap, the company that owns Snapchat, is so aggressive with updating the app. As a result, Snapchat is ever-evolving, so much that it can be hard to keep up with how it works even if you're a regular user.

What is Snap score?

Snap score is the figure shown on your Snapchat profile by your username. The score represents all your activities on the app, including the people you add, the stories you share, the snaps you send and receive.

However, note that the Snap score differs from the Snap streaks you have with your friends.

How does Snap score work?

The Snap score shows how active someone is on the platform. It's, however, different from the "Like" feature on many social apps because it doesn't represent your post's popularity.

What are the benefits of a great Snap score? Earlier, a Snapchat user could unlock trophies based on their total score. The trophies were in the form of emojis and specific filters. Snapchat replaced the trophies with charms.

The charms are more exciting and usually appear on your friend's profile. It shows the similarities that you have with your friend. For example, if you share a zodiac sign with your friend, a little charm will be displayed on their profile. However, charms are simply for fun; they don't have any practical purpose.

What increases Snap score?

The Snap score is purely for fun, but that does not mean it isn't worth increasing. There are several ways to boost your Snap score, and they include:

1. Sharing more Snapchat stories

Does Snap score go up from stories? Yes, anytime you share content to your story, you boost the Snap score. You'll also get points for checking out your friend's stories.

2. Watching videos in the “Discover” section

The “Discover” section usually has random videos from people globally. Watching them will boost your score considerably.

3. Sending are receiving snaps

Sending snaps to your friends will increase your score. Likewise, you'll get a boost in scores when you receive snaps. The fantastic thing about receiving or sending snaps is that it boosts your and your friend's scores.

Does your Snap score go up when you receive a Snap or when you open it? If you get many snaps but don't open them often, the score will still go up as long the snaps you're sending are being opened. But if you don't open the purple or red snaps you receive and fail to send any snaps, your scores won't increase.

4. Following celebrities

You can increase your Snap score by following celebrities such as soccer players and sending them snaps. Adding celebrities to your list will boost your score; you'll get one score for each celebrity. It's also a win-win, as celebrities post exciting stuff you'll enjoy watching.

5. Joining Snapchat lines

Another way to boost your Snap score is by joining the Snapchat series. It's a line of people who send snaps to each other daily for some days. Some Snapchat series run for 100 days or more. You'll boost your score significantly if you stay long enough in the series, like three days.

Do group chats increase Snap score?

Yes, group chats increase Snap score. However, sending multiple snaps to people through groups doesn't increase your score more than once. It means you'll get the same score as sending it to a single person.

How do Snapchat spam groups work?

The term "Snapchat spam groups" has been popularized by Reddit. It enables Snapchat users to join spammed groups with snaps to increase their scores. These groups aren't for bonding but to specifically improve Snapchat scores.

It's easy to join a Snapchat spam group on Reddit. All you have to do is post your Snap in a particular Reddit forum, and someone will add you as a friend to the group.

Does Snap score go up from chats?

Your score will only increase when you send video and photo snaps. The text messages you send to friends don’t matter. In addition, you won’t be awarded points for sending the same snap to multiple Snapchat users. It must be unique for you to get a point.

Do people buy Snapchat scores?

Several shady websites claim to sell Snapchat scores, but that's a lie. A 3rd party app or website can't increase your score without interacting with your Snapchat account. But if it does, Snapchat will detect suspicious activities and flag the accounts involved, including yours.
