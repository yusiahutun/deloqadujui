Super Bowl: Ice Spice's 'creepy' upside-down cross upsets fans, but it's not that deep

Ice Spice and Taylor Swift are now pretty much besties, but it doesn’t mean she’ll avoid scrutiny for what she’s wearing at the Super Bowl, and fans were upset over her cross chain necklace at the big game last night.

She was among the long list of celebrity fans who splashed out a large amount of cash to watch Travis Kelce and the Kansas City Chiefs face the San Francisco 49ers at the NFL championship last night. Ice Spice joined other star-studded guests in a VIP suite to watch the Super Bowl, but it was her cross necklace that caused as much of a stir than the results. Standing next to Taylor Swift, she was in the luxury suite with Travis’ family and friends cheering them on. Although she didn’t get ‘squished’ like Lana Del Rey did, eagle-eyed football fans weren’t happy.

Ice Spice’s cross chain necklace upsets Super Bowl fans

The reason viewers were so upset, is because they saw Ice Spice wearing an upside-down cross, with Twitter users claiming it was bringing ‘bad energy’ and called it ‘creepy’.

The upside-down cross has been used as an anti-Christian and Satanic symbol in the past, but what fans don’t realize is that it’s also an accessory in fashion, just because some people use this symbol, doesn’t mean Ice Spice and others are as well.

The Cross of Saint Peter is an inverted Latin cross, also upside down, and that represents Christianity. But it’s only been in recent times when the inverted cross has been linked to Satanism. Therefore, not all occurrences of an upside-down cross definitely send an anti-Christian message.

Despite this, because of popular culture, many thought the celebrity was sending this message – when in reality, she was just dressed in a killer ‘fit. It was designed by Alex Moss, and a BACKR99MS Concept Chain.

Not only that, but it may have been a good luck charm, because the Kansas City Chiefs beat the 49ers 25-22 in overtime. Mecole Hardman’s touchdown helped reveal the champions with just three seconds left in the game.
