1. Rapids Camp Sagana Kenya

This place is deservedly called one of the most beautiful places in Kenya. Located near a peaceful river, Rapids Camp is a perfect place for everyone who loves adventures and active time-spending activities. Rapids Camp Sagana Kenya has everything you need for a perfect camping weekend - convenient places for tents, fun outdoor activities, a tasty buffet, and fun entertainment for children.

Prepare your cameras as Rapids Camp Sagana Kenya is located in a picturesque place with scenic landscapes. Simply place your tent near the refreshing waters of the nearby Tana River and enjoy the variety of water sports - kayaking, river trekking, water challenges, white water rafting, and more.

2. Camping in Naivasha, Malewa Bush Ventures

Naivasha camping sites are one of the most popular destinations among camping lovers and fans of eco-tourism. Situated near the scenic Naivasha, next to the Kigio Wildlife Conservancy, Malewa is one of the most beautiful places for camping at a comparatively reasonable price. Besides the amazing landscapes and wild nature, Camp Malewa Bush Ventures offers its visitors team building activities, a huge variety of events, conferences, camping, adventure, and fun.

One of the advantages is that Malewa Bush Ventures is located only two hours away from Nairobi in a stunning 60-acre land on the Malewa River in the Great Rift Valley, Kenya. However, what can you do in a place like that? Don't worry, the camp administration offers a rich variety of active outdoor activities like rock climbing, high and low ropes courses, boating, fishing, programs for youth, team building programs that will break the ice in any collective, and many more.

8. Hell’s Gate Gorge and National Park

If you want to visit cheap camping sites in Naivasha, Hell's Gate Gorge and National Park is exactly what you need. The entry fee is only 300 Ksh for Kenyan citizen adults and 600 Ksh for residents. The key feature of the park is that it has a unique wildlife observation opportunity - African buffalo, zebra, eland, hartebeest, Thomson's gazelle, baboons, klipspringer antelope and Chandler’s mountain reedbuck can be met here.

The main activities of the park are:

  • Biking
  • Bird watching
  • Camping
  • Game viewing
  • Hiking
  • Horse riding
  • Rock climbing

Besides that, there is a unique opportunity to take some breathtaking pictures of the towering cliffs, water-gouged gorges, stark rock towers, scrub-clad volcanoes, and belching plumes of geothermal steam. Forget about everyday problems and relax a bit in the hot springs. Visit the Olkaria Geothermal Station or the Mervyn Carnelley Raptor Hide for an unforgettable experience. Moreover, tourist circuits, nature trails, and health spas are available for all guests.

9. Kongoni Camp

If you love the mountain views, then Kongoni Camp was founded for you. It is located less than two kilometers from Nanyuki Town, northwest of Mt Kenya off Meru Road. Here, you will be able to enjoy the breathtaking views of the Mount Kenya every morning. There are also different game parks both for children and adults.

There is an opportunity to book a single room or double room to increase the comfort of your weekend even more. If you want to be totally private, there is an opportunity to book a whole cottage that is simple yet very stylishly decorated and equipped with everything that you might need.

Of course, there is a bar with refreshing drinks and a restaurant where a professional chef will cook local cuisine - from mind-blowing grills, vegetarian dishes, homemade Italian-like pizzas, to homemade bread and cakes. Everything is prepared only from the fresh ingredients collected from the local farms.

10. Kiboko Luxury Camp

Camping in Naivasha can be luxurious if you decide to book a place at Kiboko Camp located on the shores of Lake Naivasha. The camp is accommodated with only 8 comfortable tents that are an ideal choice for two lovebirds ON their first honeymoon. For the past six years, Kiboko Luxury Camp has won over 7 awards, including two Bronze Eco-Rated Awards.

Kiboko is a perfect place for those who want to see all the beauty of the countryside. It is not surprising that you would be offered first-class meals at the local restaurant and bar combined with a stunning overview of a fast-flowing river visited by various kinds of animals from the wild.

Every tent is accommodated with a private deck, iPod docking station, flat-screen TV, and even an espresso machine.
