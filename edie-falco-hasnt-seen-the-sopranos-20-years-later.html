Edie Falco Says She Still Hasnt Seen The Sopranos 20 Years After The Series Premiered

February 06, 2024
blog

The Sopranos is the one television show everybody has been talking about this week, and that’s because it has been 20 years since the series premiered. That’s right, two decades since James Gandolfini’s character of Tony Soprano became legendary. But guess what? There’s one cast-member that actually hasn’t seen the show at all — Edie Falco.

Edie, 55, and other players from the iconic HBO show all got together on Wednesday, Jan. 9 at the SVA Theater in New York City for a special 20th anniversary panel discussion. The actors took time to look back at the show that changed the landscape of gritty television dramas. But Edie probably can’t relate since she hasn’t watched yet. “I haven’t seen the show, actually,” the Nurse Jackie alum revealed to Extra. “There are many, many episodes I haven’t seen.”

Edie also touched on just how strange it is to see all of her old co-stars again. “It’s completely surreal,” she told the outlet. “It’s just weird. It was so nice to see everybody and have a moment of like, ‘Oh, this is really weird!’ At least we can all be open about the fact that we all had a certain amount of trepidation about showing up. Like, what is this going to feel like, you know?” Edie added, “Many of these people I haven’t seen in 10, 15 years.”

Edie, who played James’ on-screen wife on the series, was also recently part of a cast interview on Today, and the actress was asked about her favorite scene with her TV husband. “First thing that comes to mind was there was a scene where he and I were in a lawn chair in the backyard by the pool,” she said. “It was tender, but it was Sopranos-esque in that it wasn’t, like, loving. It was just very, sort of, a day in the life of a long-term couple.”

Well, we think it’s only right that someone let Edie know where she can see the show that won her two Golden Globes. Although, if someone has the DVD boxed set they can dust off, feel free to let her know, too.
