Who is JT from My 600-Lb Life?

Julius 'JT' Clark participated in the eighth season of the TLC reality TV show, appearing in episode four in 2020. He was 32 (35 years in 2023) and living with his dog and girlfriend in Owasso, Oklahoma.

JT was addicted to food and couldn't stop eating despite being alarmingly obese and not knowing his weight. He lost his job aged 25 due to his obese weight, which was 700 pounds (317 kgs), and made him unable to stand for long.

He was 892 lbs and had a 100-pound swelling (lymphedema) on his leg. His addiction to food and rapid weight gain prompted him to contact Dr Younan Nowzaradan, aka Dr Now, who had helped other obese patients lose weight.
