The Band Originated in San Diego, California

Pierce The Veil was formed in 2006 in San Diego, California, by brothers Vic and Mike Fuentes. The band's early years were marked by their relentless dedication to perfecting their craft and creating a distinct musical identity.

Their Debut Album, "A Flair for the Dramatic," Garnered Critical Acclaim

Released in 2007, "A Flair for the Dramatic" showcased the band's emotive lyrics and powerful instrumentation. It received widespread praise from music critics and laid the foundation for their future success.

The Band's Name Holds Personal Significance

The name "Pierce The Veil" is derived from a concept in the 2007 film "Donnie Darko," symbolizing a moment of clarity. This name reflects the band's desire to evoke deep emotions and provoke introspection through their music.
