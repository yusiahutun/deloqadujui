Cubone is a Ground-type Pokémon.

Cubone is known for its distinct appearance and is classified as a Ground-type Pokémon in the popular franchise. It is primarily found in mountainous regions and caves.

It wears the skull of its deceased mother.

Cubone is a solitary Pokémon.

Cubone evolves into Marowak.
