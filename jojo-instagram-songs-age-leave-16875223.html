You wont believe what Get Out singer JoJo looks like now

July 27, 2024
blog

Many fans will be wondering where is JoJo now?

This comes as the one-time chart-topper has appeared to completely disappear from the spotlight in recent years, following a string of mega hits in the early 2000’s.

JoJo (Joanna Noëlle Lévesque) who hails from Massachusetts, had her first taste of the spotlight back in 2003 when she competed on America’s Most Talented Kids.

She was soon signed by Background Records and released her debut album JoJo in June 2004 at the tender age of 13.

Her debut single Leave (Get Out) stormed the charts, reaching number one in the US Billboard Pop songs chart.

The teen’s second album, The High Rood, was released in October 2006.

Mega catchy track Too Little Too Late from the album, was released in August the same year and scored the number three spot on the Billboard Hot 100.

The second song on the album experienced considerably less success, only peaking at number 76 on the Billboard Hot 100.

The track that followed; Anything, also experienced limited success.

Following a string of covers such as T-Pain’s Can’t Believe It, JoJo took a five-year hiatus from music, until returning to the charts in 2011, with song Disaster.

The song only managed to secure the number 87 spot in the Billboard 100, and fell off the charts the following week.

The pop princess looked quite the brunette bombshell in the pic, and was lavished with praised from her following, as she proved that she is back with a bang.
